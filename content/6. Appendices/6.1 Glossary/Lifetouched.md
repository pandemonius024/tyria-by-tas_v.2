Lifetouching is a process through which a typically non-sentient creature becomes sentient. Lifetouched creatures can exhibit any amount of sentience ranging from recognizing their own name, to possessing a personality completely indistinguishable from a creature that was living from birth. (In this case, "living from birth" refers to having the spark of life present from its inception).
