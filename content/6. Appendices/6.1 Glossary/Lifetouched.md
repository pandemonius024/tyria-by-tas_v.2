"Lifetouched", adj., refers to any automaton, construct, summon, familiar, or some other typically non-sentient object, that has gained sentience through a blessing, magical means, or some other extraordinary circumstance. 

Objects may become lifetouched on purpose, like by being baptized in [[Tyria's gift]], or being willed so by [[Pharasma, the inevitable|Pharasma]]. Objects may also become lifetouched randomly at any point. Several [[Warforged]] became lifetouched before, during, or after their deployment in [[Dawnguard]]'s war against the high elves. Several [[Axiomites]] have been lifetouched.
