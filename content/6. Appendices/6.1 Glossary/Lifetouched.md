"Lifetouched" is an adjective, referring to any automaton, construct, summon, familiar, or some other typically non-sentient object, that gains sentience through a blessing, magic, or some other extraordinary mean. An object may be lifetouched by, for example, being submerged in [[Tyria's gift]], or being targeted by [[Pharasma, the inevitable|Pharasma]].

Objects may become lifetouched randomly at any point. Several [[Warforged]] became lifetouched before, during, or after their deployment in [[Dawnguard]]'s war against the high elves. Several [[Axiomites]] have been lifetouched, usually resulting in their resetting, scrapping, or escape.
