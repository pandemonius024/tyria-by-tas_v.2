> *"I like to think of it as a living chronicle of Tyria's greatest."*
> - The Ashen Padma

*Ecliptic Heroes* is a cross-planar trading card game, and the pet project of [[The Ashen Padma]]. In making *Ecliptic Heroes*, The Ashen Padma has collated and illustrated information about the people that The Ashen Padma considers influential. The Ashen Padma has not revealed what criterion The Ashen Padma use to determine influence.

*Ecliptic Heroes* is distributed by a network of extra-planar bags, each connected to the same pile of *Ecliptic Heroes* cards. The Ashen Padma supplies this extra-planar space with cards, and the cards are distributed to buyers and collectors via the aforementioned bags. The cards are given with an unknown rarity curve. Limited to 1 card per 1 collector per 1 day.
## Specifications and entry criterion
Each card is a 3.5x6.0x0.1 in. beveled chromium rectangular prism, with laser-etched illustrations done, by eye(s), by The Ashen Padma. The chromium is harvested directly from the island that The Ashen Padma lives on. On the back of every card is a full size etching of The Ashen Padma's garden. The front of every card has a 2/3 size illustration of the subject, with their name and flavor text taking up the rest of the space.

The Ashen Padma uses plant life grown from The Ashen Padma's garden to discern the identity of the most influential individuals to Tyria's history. On occasion, denizens of [[Dweomerheart]] can see smoke rising from The Ashen Padma's garden, indicating that The Ashen Padma has determined another entry into *Ecliptic Heroes*. 

A person's rarity in *Ecliptic Heroes* is directly proportional to their influence; the greater the deeds, the greater the rarity of their associated card.
## Drawing a card
When a creature draws a card from any of the available drop bags, they first roll a d100 to determine the rarity of their card. Then, they roll the specified dice to determine what card is drawn. 

Some cards have multiple variations attached to them. In these cases, roll the indicated dice to determine which variation is pulled.
