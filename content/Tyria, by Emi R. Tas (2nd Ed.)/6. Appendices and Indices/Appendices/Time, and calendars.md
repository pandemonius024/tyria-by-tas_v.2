En media, Tyria's calendar follows her looping around Lunaris and Solaris. The naming convention was penned by scriptors of the [[Eternal Empire]], and behaves similarly to the Gregorian calendar of us Earthlings.

The year 1 corresponds to the year that Pharasma, the inevitable split the Prime Material Plane, suffixed by the index AS, meaning "After Shattering"; BS, "Before Shattering". See: [[Timeline of major events]] for a chronological look at the world of Tyria.

The names of the days correspond to what the Eternals considered to be fundamental components of Eternal society:
- Saturday :: Solaro - the Sun, in reference to Solaris;
- Sunday :: Lunaro - the Moon, in reference to Lunaris;
- Monday :: Fiero - fire, heat, cooking;
- Tuesday :: Glacio - ice, water;
- Wednesday :: Galvano - the clang of hot iron on steel;
- Thursday :: Kaso - lit. life;
- Friday :: Sacrato - rites of grace, sacrifice.

The names of the months of the year correspond to honored historical figures:
- Eterni (January);
- Atziri (February);
- Vivici (March);
- Lurici (April);
- Sagari, (May);
- Vitali (June);
- Azmeri (July);
- Divini (August);
- Derivi (September);
- Phreci (October);
- Caspiri (November);
- Astrali (December).
