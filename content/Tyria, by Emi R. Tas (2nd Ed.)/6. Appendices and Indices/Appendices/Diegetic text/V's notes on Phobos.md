#ETERNITY 

*[[Ambrose Tenebris]] has taken extensive notes on [[Phobos Tenebris]] after the incident that killed the original [[Deimos Tenebris]]. The file itself has Phobos' name printed on it; Deimos is handwritten next to it. Here are a few choice excerpts:*

Caspiri (Nov), 3rd Glacio (Tue) of 603, under the Autumnal moon:
- Deimos pressed, loses control of \[excised\].
- Phobos passed away. Cause of death: consumption by Pit Fiend, originated from \[excised\] opening.
- Deimos flees, shadow exhibits sentience.
- Shadow last seen comforting Deimos.

Caspiri (Nov), 3rd Sacrato (Fri) of 603, under the Autumnal moon:
- Deimos sighted, traveling from home village, due Curos.
- Shadow is corporeal, manifest as a clone of Phobos.
- Shadow exhibits humanoid-qualities.

Astrali (Dec), 1st Lunaro (Sun) of 603, under the Winter moon:
- Deimos sighted, under convulsions.
- Shadow loses material form momentarily.
- Local priest stabilizes Deimos, sights Shadow.
- Shadow instability remains, eliminates priest.
- Deimos travels to [[Trogal]].

Astrali (Dec), 1st Fiero (Mon) of 603, under the Winter moon:
- Deimos arrives in Trogal.
- Shadow resumed Phobos' form
- Shadow exhibits potential to enter other objects' shadows.
- Shadow successfully acquires a bundle of tomatoes.
- Deimos eats, Shadow watches.

Astrali (Dec), 2nd Fiero (Mon) of 603, under the Winter moon:
- Deimos under convulsions.
- Shadow unable to remain, temporarily dissipates.
- Deimos retains control shortly after Shadow's disappearance.
- Deimos seen huddled inside of a well, Shadow reappears.

(The following few years detail your experiences running town to town, stealing from farmers, and attempting to find refuge. It details your slow drift apart from Phobos.)

Sagari (May), 4th Kaso (Thu) of 608, under the Summer moon:
- Deimos sighting in Asteria, appears pale and malnourished.
- Shadow (henceforth referred to as Phobos) sighted in [[Leni]], fumbled theft.
- Shadow intercepted by Viscan, recruited for further observation.

(The following years are sparse on information on Deimos. They detail his successful hits, and give location and context for each elimination.)

Divini (Aug), 3rd Kaso (Thu) of 631, under the Autumnal moon:
- Deimos sighted in Vinovia.
- Appearance is diminutive, showing signs of extreme malnutrition. Color drained from skin, bones stabbing through to the outside. Shadow is missing, despite sun's presence.
- Appears to be unresponsive, stares firmly in one direction.
- Retains combat prowess and stealth abilities.

Divini (Aug), 4th Galvano (Wed) of 631, under the Autumnal moon:
- Phobos completed work in Asteria.
- Deimos sighted again in Vinovia under convulsions.
- Shadows trailing out of orifices, latching onto nearby sands.
- Approximated 2.5 meter radius circle formed by shadows, research indicates potential to link to the Shadowfell.
- Artifact on Deimos glares, halts shadows.

Derivi (Sep), 1st Solaro (Sat) of 631, under the Autumnal moon:
- Decision to file hit on Deimos. Phobos undertakes.
