The Eclipse refers to the day in which [[Solaris]] fell from [[Celestia]], allegedly slain by [[Lunaris]]. The Eclipse is widely considered a cataclysmic event, in which the sun no longer rose, and the resulting lack of protection from the darkness led to lengthened shadows, and widespread distrust. The echoes of this sisterly betrayal resounded through Tyria; most secluded themselves, focusing inwards, and just keeping on trying to survive. The nights became cold with a bitter angst, while the days hung dry and dark without her shining light.
