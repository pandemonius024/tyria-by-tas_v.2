The Tears of Tyria are a collection of three jewels: the [[Tear of Time]], the [[Tear of Space]], and the [[Tear of Life]]. These jewels, made of coagulated [[Luminiferous Aether]], fell from Tyria's cheek as she wept over the corpses of her children, the Divinarius. The Tears posses plane-shaping abilities corresponding to their namesake. For example, the Tear of Time has been used across multiple instances to branch timelines, most notably by Nox in the diversion of the Eclipse at the end of the ECLIPSE campaign. The Tears of Tyria, as they are in myth, were said to have spun the wheels of space, time, and life, marking the beginning of history.

In the Solsticed timeline, Dante du Fleur fashioned [[Eternity]] out of the Tears of Tyria, a process that killed Iron Fist Alexander, and threw himself with Eternity into the Void. There, Dante remains.
