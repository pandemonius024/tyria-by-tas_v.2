Lupa Lumula is an enclave of ethnic Wraeclastians within [[Dawnguard]]. Lupa Lumula was formed shortly after Wraeclast's sinking into the sea. The residents of Lupa Lumula are primarily those who wished for a better life outside of the clutches of [[Oriath]]'s stone walls and armored streets; it is named for a revolutionary who dared die on her feet while she stood against the Ebony Legion.
