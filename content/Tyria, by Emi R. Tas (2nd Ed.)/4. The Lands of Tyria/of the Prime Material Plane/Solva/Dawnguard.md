#SUNDER 

Dawnguard is the capital city of the human-occupied territories of [[Solva]]. Dawnguard was built onto the southwestern shores of Solva as a shrine of sorts, due west of the bottommost mountains of the Ashen Peaks. Dawnguard was later built up and fortified as a pivotal strategic hold in the human struggle against the high elves of the Peaks, and has grown into the trading and cultural epicenter of human-Solva. 

Dawnguard was built up from its humble-farmland roots in the midst of the religious fervor spurred on by Tangmazu, the trickster. Once Tangmazu twisted the story of the Dancing Sisters into one of warring sisters, humans quickly picked a side and worshiped her. [[Spiral Castle]], the centerpiece of Dawnguard, was built up, quote "to reach [[Solaris]]' height, \[and\] to hold her up in her fight."

For centuries following, human Solvans began to look fondly upon the high elves' territory, and endeavored to snag their mountains for themselves; an exploit further fueled by the rumors of bastilium underground. In this endeavor, Dawnguard evolved from a religious, cultural hub into a devastating war machine whose citizens were sent roaring into the Peaks.
