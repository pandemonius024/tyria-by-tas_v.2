Dawnguard is the capital city of the human-occupied territories of [[Solva]]. Dawnguard was built onto the southwestern shores of Solva as a shrine of sorts, due west of the bottommost mountains of the Ashen Peaks. Dawnguard was later built up and fortified as a pivotal strategic hold in the human struggle against the high elves of the Peaks, and has grown into the trading and cultural epicenter of human-Solva. 

Dawnguard was built up from its humble-farmland roots in the midst of the religious fervor spurred on by Tangmazu, the trickster. Once Tangmazu twisted the story of the Dancing Sisters into one of warring sisters, humans quickly picked a side and worshiped her. Spiral Castle, the centerpiece of Dawnguard, was built up, quote "to reach [[Solaris]]' height, \[and\] to hold her up in her fight."

For centuries following, human Solvans began to look fondly upon the high elves' territory, and endeavored to snag their mountains for themselves; an exploit further fueled by the rumors of bastilium underground. In this endeavor, Dawnguard evolved from a religious, cultural hub into a devastating war machine whose citizens were sent roaring into the Peaks.
## Spiral Castle
Spiral Castle is a monumental stone spire, built in a conical-horn shape. The base has a diameter of 400 ft., and Spiral stretches upwards, of 2000 ft. A staircase in Spiral's dead center reaches ~1800 ft. high, leaving any who wish to reach the peak to climb the remaining 200 ft. of spiral. Spiral Castle is the centerpiece of Dawnguard, hosting the monarch, their governing body, and a vast gathering area surrounded by food stands, buskers, and the passerby. All roads can navigate to Spiral, making it the single most populace intersection for foot traffic.
## Lupa Lumula
Lupa Lumula is an enclave of ethnic Wraeclastians within [[Dawnguard]]. Lupa Lumula was formed shortly after Wraeclast's sinking into the sea. The residents of Lupa Lumula are primarily those who wished for a better life outside of the clutches of [[Oriath]]'s stone walls and armored streets; it is named for a revolutionary who dared die on her feet while she stood against the Ebony Legion.
## History
- Following the SUNDER campaign, Arthas tailed an elf from his dreams into the annals of Spiral Castle. Within, he tailed the elf through a doorway leading him and his allies into the Realm of Chaos. After a terse few exchanges, and an abyssal fight, Arthas and company were returned to Spiral Castle unharmed -- with a new blade, as well.
- [[Quzahn]] runs an orphanage out of Dawnguard, where he teaches defenseless children how to fight on their own.
