#SUNDER 

A lakeside city, located on eastern [[Solva]], Vin Arbor is a humble logging village. It is often described as one, if not *the* most beautiful city in Solva. Its mayor, [[Cassius Clay]], is a humble man of logging origins, who wants for the best for his people. 

From Callum, of the Black Rose:
> *"Cassius Clay is a humble, benevolent Lord who desires nothing but the best for his citizens. He presides over the trading city Vin Arbor, and fights for continued equality and opportunity in his people. His efforts are breathtaking. Vin Arbor is easily the most beautiful city in Solva (don't tell the Queen), with a system of governance that benefits the workers the most. We suspect the Unseen have taken a hold within the Verdant forest to his west, and are plotting their attack. Your job would be to explore the woodland, sniff them out, and snuff their plan."*
