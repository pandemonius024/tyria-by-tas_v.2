Baveil is a small city on the eastern half of [[Solva]], renowned by the surrounding townships for its bustling artisanal district. Baveil is home to the likes of Armus, a conjuration inscriber who worshipped Solaris, and Sarcerian, a pet/familiar armorer. Baveil once housed the Black Rose Company, prior to its relocation.

In the REAVE campaign, the party met at Baveil to rendezvous with the Black Rose Company. Following a run-in with Rox, the Black Rose Company's HQ was compromised, incentivizing the Company to move house from Baveil to Blackwater.
