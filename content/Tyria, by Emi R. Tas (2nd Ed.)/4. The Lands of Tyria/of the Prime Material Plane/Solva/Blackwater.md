Blackwater is a port town on the eastern reaches of [[Solva]]. Blackwater serves as both, an intermediate logistic step in overseas trade, and a safehouse for the pirates who raid those traders. The citizens and municipality alike are willing to overlook the illegal affairs of Blackwater's pirates, by virtue of the sheer amount of coin that both businesses draw. Blackwater levies taxes on trading companies moving product in their many docks; trading companies seek mercenaries to protect their goods against pirates; pirates pay off dockhands to harbor their ships and peddle their wares. Its docks are well-equipped to handle large volumes of goods, its roads are well-paved and readily stocked with horse and carriage, and sellswords ready to protect against theft are common in the area. Despite the steep fees that trading companies face there, Blackwater has remained a high-ranking destination for all goods entering or exiting Solva.
