Solva is the third largest continent on the [[Material Plane]], and is home to a seemingly-endless conflict between the high elves of Elkmire, and the humans of Dawnguard. Solva is most known for its independence from the Revanon-dominated global trading market. Propelled by its civil and inter-national wars, the various states of Solva have generated a self-sufficient industry of its own, wages wars of its own, and operates, desirably, on its own.
