Wraeclast is no longer a continent of the [[Material Plane]], after being summarily sunk into the water by Torentia, following the Solstice. Torentia, in response to the growing threat that Kitava, the insatiable presented to greater Tyria, flooded the lands of Wraeclast. In doing so, Kitava and his supporters have been drowned away, leaving nothing but a handful of disparate islands to dot the seascape that was Wraeclast.
