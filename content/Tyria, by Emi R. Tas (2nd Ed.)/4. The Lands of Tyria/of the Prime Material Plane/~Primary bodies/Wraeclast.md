Wraeclast was a continent in the [[Material Plane]], losing its continental status after being sunk into the water by [[Torentia]], following the Solstice. Torentia was motivated to destroy the continent of Wraeclast after signs of [[Kitava, the insatiable]]'s immediate return became apparent. In doing so, Kitava and his supporters have been drowned away, leaving nothing but a handful of disparate islands to dot the seascape that was Wraeclast.
