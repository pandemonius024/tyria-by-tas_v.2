Revanon is the largest continent on the [[Material Plane]], and is home to a number of independent states. Revanon houses the likes of Asteria, a global trading port, centralized on its promotion of the arts -- to a decadent degree; Axis, a newly-minted state of Revanon, shipped in from the Shadowfell with its twisted ruler, Mania; and Amsa, an independent, roaming city of vices, which peruses the deserts of Vinovia searching for marks, and magics. 

Revanon has been determined to be the continent upon which the first creatures resembling humanoids appeared. The oldest, confirmed humanoid skeleton was found on the steps of the Vardousia Range: one of the Dwarves, in 6,250 BS. The earliest signs of human civilization appeared at Mor Proxima, estimated to have been settled in 6,250 BS, as well. Since that time, the denizens of Revanon have scoured its lands for its most fruitful arbors, most lucrative minerals, and most desirable living quarters.
