Axis is a city located on the northern reaches of [[E'Valengues]]. Axis is located just south of the [[Vasiliča Range]], and is surrounded by dense forest. Axis was originally [[Mania]]'s stronghold in the [[Shadowfell]]; after the [[Solstice]], Axis was superimposed over [[E'Lenora]], effectively occupying the elven settlement.
## Creation and the Solstice
Axis began as a hidey hole for Mania just following her banishment into the Shadowfell. After [[Pharasma, the inevitable|Pharasma]] divided the planes into 3, Mania was left with nothing but her image of the [[Hill]]. Mania used the materials present in the Hill to construct somewhat of a shelter for herself in the early days, and would later use the materials to construct the first [[Axiomites]]. Together with the Axiomites, Mania grew Axis.

Axis was built radially out from the Hill; as Mania's knowledge of the Shadowfell grew, so too did Axis' borders. While the city appears to be a collection of tightly packed residences, in reality it is a series of nested defenses, each layer dedicated to defending against one type of threat from the Shadowfell.

When the Solstice merged the 3 planes back together, Axis was brought to the Material atop E'Lenora. As luck would have it, the structures immediately surrounding the Hill in both E'Lenora and Axis overlapped within, a few percentage points of error. Little to no buildings had to be squished or slid into place, preserving both E'Lenora and Axis' original geometry. 

Unfortunately for E'Lenora, the more militant Axis was well-equipped and well-prepared for a sudden takeover of E'Lenora; E'Lenora's original occupants were wiped out the same night Axis arrived.
## Layout
Axis uses a radial and circular road layout, a series of concentric circular roads connected by radial roads. In the heart of Axis is the Hill, a million-pound drop hammer; surrounding the Hill is E'Lenora, an elven settlement under occupation by Axis. Expanding out from E'Lenora are layers of defenses originally built for use against the Shadowfell, culminating in a sheer-faced, sloping wall encasing the entire city.

The occupied E'Lenorans are allowed to remain in their original houses, but are restricted to the confines of Axis' walls. The Axiomites reside within various maintenance centers distributed evenly throughout Axis.
