Axis is [[Mania]]'s vision of a complete and productive city. Axis was created by Mania in isolation within the [[Shadowfell]], and following the [[Solstice]], was brought to the Material. Immediately after the Solstice, Axis was planted atop [[E'Lenora]], centered on the [[Hill]]. The city layout is best described as hostile to outsiders.
