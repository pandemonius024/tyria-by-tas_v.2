Serpent's Pass is an isthmus over the Unending Sea, connecting the south of [[Vinovia]] to the northeast of [[E'Valengues]]. Serpent's Pass has earned its moniker by virtue of its proximity to sea dragon-populated waters, and the ease with which those sea dragons can access travelers within the Pass.

In the ECLIPSE campaign, the pets of ECLIPSE's various members go off on a separate journey, to check in on [[E'Lenora]] ahead of the party. Along they way, they find that the Pass had become territorialized by Soldiers, who had established a barricade around the Pass. Additionally, the ECLIPSE pet party came across a sect Soldier splinter cell, helmed by Tirion who will, in a few days' time, be killed by the party.
