The Hill is a massive drop hammer created and used by [[Pharasma, the inevitable]]. It weighs well over 1 million pounds, is 10 stories tall in total, and its supporting machinery is so extensive, that it creates the rounded, sloping visage for which the Hill is named. The Hill was used by Pharasma in the year 0 AS to divide the [[Prime Material Plane]] into 3 parts in an event known as the [[Shattering]]. In conjunction with the [[Tears of Tyria]], Pharasma used the Hill to crowbar her way into the [[Luminiferous Aether]], and create [[Alethia]].

In the centuries since the formation of Alethia, the Hill lay dormant and unused. As wood elves search for greener pastures, a faction of wood elves land upon the Hill and interpret it as a beacon from the gods. The wood elves marveled at the relatively alien construction before them, and are inspired to fashion a civilization modeled after the engineering exhibited in the Hill. [[E'Lenora]] is born.

Meanwhile, immediately after the Shattering, in the newly-minted [[Shadowfell]], is one isolated [[Mania]]. The Hill played an important role in Mania's imprisonment and survival, being both the tool used to send her into a plane of her own, as well as the resource used by her to defend herself. The first [[Axiomites]] are fashioned out of the remnants of the Hill that traveled with her into the Shadowfell, enabling her to snowball into the creation of [[Axis]].
