The Vasiliča Range is a mountain range in [[Revanon]] dividing both Vinovia and Metrica Province from E'Valengues. The Vasiliča Range stretches from the eastern reaches of F'mdell, due east onwards to Serpent's Pass. The Vasiliča Range is heavily monitored by a deluge of scouts from E'Lenora. The Vasiliča Range is populated by a number of unexplained, dead-end caves, one of which Agnar rested in following his close shave with Damian Ashborne's cavalcade.
