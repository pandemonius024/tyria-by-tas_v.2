The Vardousia Range is an expansive mountain range in [[Revanon]] that is home to the Eternal Blades, and La Escuela de la Montanha. Underneath the Vardousia Range, is an equally-expansive network of caves that once housed the Dwarves.

The Vardousia Range is frequently quarried by industries in Asteria, who have accurately appraised the range as being rich with minerals and metals typically found deeper within Tyria's crust. The parties of ETERNITY and ASCENSION, separately, ventured into quarries into the Vardousia Range, discovering rust monsters, living stalagmites, and other hostile rocks and metals.
