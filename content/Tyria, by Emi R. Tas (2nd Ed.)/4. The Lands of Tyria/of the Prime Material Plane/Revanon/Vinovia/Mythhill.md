#ETERNITY 

Mythhill is a humble village near the northeastern shore of [[Vinovia]]. There is a hill nearby.

In the ETERNITY campaign, the denizens of Mythhill were placed under an enchantment that caused the villagers to attempt to capture Rox and/or Lucia on sight. This enchantment was placed unto them by the devil [[Lars]], in hopes of tormenting Rox further. ETERNITY, wary of Lars' intentions, managed to subvert Lars' plan by redirecting Rox and Lucia to neighboring [[Duskgrove]].
