Green Bay Plaza is a square constructed on topside [[Amsa]], an uncharacteristically clean and formal area of the walking city. The Plaza is a horseshoe-shaped rink, with food stands, entertainment, and all sorts of party-enabling accoutrement. The hole in the center is rimmed with an automated anti-gravity net, allowing Plaza visitors to leap off into the desert, and be whisked safely back to where they lept. The Plaza is surrounded on all sides by aggressive fans and air conditioning, covered in flowing silks and generous awnings, and provides denizens with a sumptuous view of the desert.

In the SOLSTICE campaign, the Green Bay Plaza hosted the 3rd annual Diver Symposium, a celebration of advancements made in engineering technology. During this Symposium, the party of SOLSTICE made contact with [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], who was in attendance while courting the favor of [[Eternos]] and their engineering minds.
