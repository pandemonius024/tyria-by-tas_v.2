Corcoran Laboratory is situated in topside [[Amsa]], and focuses its efforts on the research and development of genetic mutations, and evolutionary branching. Corcoran Laboratory was temporarily taken by force by [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], in the lead-up to SOLSTICE with the explicit goal of developing bio-weapons capable of dominating the world. Following the SOLSTICE campaign, Corcoran Laboratory was liberated from Tangmazu's influence, and business as usual resumed.
