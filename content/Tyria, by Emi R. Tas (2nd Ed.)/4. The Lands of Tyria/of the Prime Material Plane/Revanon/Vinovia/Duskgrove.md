Duskgrove is a village on the northeastern shore of [[Vinovia]].

In the ETERNITY campaign, Qatar passed by Duskgrove on his way out of Lex Lutetia, a neighboring prison. Later, the party of ETERNITY visited Duskgrove while attempting to intercept Rox and Lucia, in hopes of diverting them from the devil Lars.
