#ETERNITY 

Pavo Perilos was a floating island in the skies above Mor Proxima, owned by [[Cassandra Lumen]] for use as a tea-room. It was suspended a mile into the air using the [[Tear of Space]], whose taking by the party of ETERNITY caused Pavo Perilos to plunge into the sands below. Lumen, after lamenting the loss of her precious tea-room, left Pavo Perilos in the sands of [[Vinovia]].

Lumen initially constructed Pavo Perilos into a clearing due southeast of E'Lenora. Following Pharasma, the inevitable's loud, messy destruction of the Prime Material, Lumen utilized the Tear of Space to move Pavo Perilos somewhere nicer. The ensuing removal of a huge chunk of earth resulted in the formation of a lake, which remains to this day.

The party of ETERNITY discovered Pavo Perilos while searching for clues as to what Anton Essa was doing. There, they discovered Orion, a young blue dragon guarding the tower, who was promptly smote into nonexistence. 

Within the walls of Pavo Perilos proper, ETERNITY would find a rather demented puzzle, involving 5 colored flowers arranged in a circle about a central, dark-blue leafed tree, and a wall of statues with their palms raised. The solution to the puzzle involved spelling out the word "LIGHT" with the 5 flowers, referring to the color of the flowers as labeled to spell out light. 

Shortly after the party solved the puzzle, Lumen gifted Dante a small, pruned version of the tree in the center of the puzzle room, which Dante named [[Lumen's Blight]].

Beyond the puzzle door, the party of ETERNITY encountered Armus, one of the corrupted Soldiers of Strife, following Damian Ashborne's orders to secure the Tear of Space. After the party slew Armus, his soul entered Agnar Lightbringer's blade, the Soultaker. 

The Tear of Space was nested in the hands of a praying monk, who disintegrated into dust as the party took the Tear of Space from them. Once the Tear was taken, Pavo Perilos plummeted to the ground, where its ruins remain.

Isko Kidar would recognize the utility in Pavo Perilos' stones, when he fashioned a portal into the Shattered wasteland for Qatar Altan's use. Thanks to the tower's proximity to E'Lenora before the time of Pharasma's division of the planes, the stones became marked with standing waves whose frequency matched that of the wasteland. Using this fact, Isko could, with Qatar's help, fashion a physical gateway of stones, which led into the wasteland. A mirror of this gate would be used by Tyria to return SOLSTICE and the Dreamwalkers to the Material.
