The Rosholm Caverns are a derelict, closed-off series of caves nearby [[Highgate]]. The Caverns were once exploited for their rare minerals years ago, but has since been closed to the public following a series of mining accidents deemed "horrific, soul-crunching, should be sealed off", by a survivor. Towards the bottom of Rosholm is a secretive bio-jeweling facility maintained by [[Dialla]], and defended by her gemlings.
