Leni is a humble village in the south-eastern reaches of [[Metrica Province]]. 

Leni was visited by Deimos' shadowy fragment, [[Phobos Tenebris]], after the destruction of [[Hollowfall]]. Phobos, or the shadow representing Phobos, had developed a functioning digestive tract, and began to require food to survive. Phobos was caught by Viscan of the [[Unseen]] here, and summarily taken under the Unseen's wing henceforth.
