Tyria's Root is a realm-shaping tree whose roots stretch far into the crust of the Prime Material Plane, and is thought to be the origin of all woodland life across Tyria. Tyria's Root stands at over 1,200 meters tall, with an average diameter of 200 meters. It is large enough to house a society of elves within its trunk, who labor to ensure the health of Tyria's Root, and the surrounding forest. Following ASCENSION, Tyria's Root is found to be plagued by a strange, purple fungi that forcibly controls the bodies of the elves it seeds upon. This fungi would be burnt out by [[Booke]] and company, liberating the elves, and healing the [[Forsaken Forest]].

In the Eclipsed timeline, Tyria's Root is poisoned by minions of [[Chaos]], who arrive at the tree from a portal to the Realm of Chaos. The SOULREND party deign to close it, fighting their way to the very top of the tree, allowing Eliza Khione to temporarily channel Lunaris' Moonstone to seal the planar rift shut. 

In the Solsticed timeline, Tyria's Root remains perfectly healthy, and the elves of Metrica get to work on repairing the damages that the fungi caused. Once the Solstice dawns on Tyria, Tyria's Root becomes a de facto base of operations for the elves, who must learn to wield their tools as weapons of war, against the forces of the Shadowfell that threaten them.
