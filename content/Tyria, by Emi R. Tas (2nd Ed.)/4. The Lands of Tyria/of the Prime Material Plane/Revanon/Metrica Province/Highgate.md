Highgate is a city built into the side of a mountain, prospering from the stone and mineral trade the city's mines produce. The mountain upon which Highgate is built is exceedingly steep, necessitating the usage of stairs, ramps, curved streets, pulley systems, and sturdy fences in its architectural design. The grand majority of Highgate's buildings are constructed facing outwards, such that the entrance and windows allow inhabitants to appreciate Highgate's unrivaled view. While buildings are restricted to the outside of the mountain, the roads, stairs, and pulley system that comprise the backbone of Highgate's logistics, are constructed using the interior of the mountain. Highgate is a constituent of [[Asteria]], and located just north of Asteria, within [[Metrica Province]].

Highgate stands as a symbol of hope and self-determination, as at the time of its creation, scant few believed a city could be built into the side of a mountain. Legends of dwarves and their unique stonemasonry abilities threw into doubt the humans' ability to build such a city. Highgate's continued existence, then, defies that doubt, and rewards the defiantly hopeful with proof of their capabilities.
## History
In the ASCENSION campaign, the party's travels brought them into Highgate, after being hailed there by a messenger at Stockfort. Once at Highgate, the party encountered a man named Shay who, within Highgate's Chrysanthemum tavern, established a rapport with the party once he affirmed their allegiances.
