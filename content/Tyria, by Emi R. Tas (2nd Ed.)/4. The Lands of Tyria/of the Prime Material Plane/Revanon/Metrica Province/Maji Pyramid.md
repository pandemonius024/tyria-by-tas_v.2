The Maji pyramid is a ruined landmark on the outskirts of [[Tolevia]], nested in the adjacent, seaside jungle. The ruin is a 120-meter tall, square pyramid constructed out of limestone, engraved with similarly square, geometric patterns resembling glyphs, and defended by a slew of ancient automaton. Within the pyramid, and under ground level, are a series of graves held sacred to the Maji. 

At the beginning of the BANE campaign, [[Niko]], an eccentric dungeon delver and ruin enjoyer rallied the party of BANE together to explore the Maji pyramid. Unbeknownst to them, a grave-robbing party formed by Asterian noble Jarl Anders was already within the pyramid, and were in the process of stealing from the dead when BANE arrived. Anders and company quickly left the scene, choosing to hastily go on with their presentation and justification of their archaeological expeditions, a thinly veiled embezzlement scheme, that would ultimately go unnoticed.
