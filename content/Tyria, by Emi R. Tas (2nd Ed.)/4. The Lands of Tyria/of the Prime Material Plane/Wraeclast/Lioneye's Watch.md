Lioneye's watch was a haphazard encampment on the southeast shore of mainland [[Wraeclast]]. As an encampment on the shore facing [[Oriath]], Lioneye's watch is the de facto landing site of people exiled from Oriath; people would be tossed into the ocean, and their bodies would shore up on Lioneye's beaches. Lioneye's watch was named after Marceus Lioneye, a general of the [[Eternal Empire]] whose final stand was made on that very shore.

At the beginning of the ASCENSION campaign, (the very first session!,) the nascent party washed ashore Lioneye's watch's beach. There, they overturned a beastly abomination named Hillock, and began their adventure.

In the ASCENSION campaign, Rox made his first appearance. He rolled up a cart half-filled with bodies, and offered the party coin for any bodies they may encounter. This offer was turned down, and Rox went on his merry way.

Following the ASCENSION campaign, Lioneye's watch was left in a somewhat worse-off state than it had begun. Despite the party's best efforts to lend their aid to the people of Lioneye's, greater forces targeted Lioneye's. Arakaali's eventually came to power uncontested, leaving the god of lust free to trample Lioneye's watch, and spin it white with her silk.

Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, Lioneye's watch was sunk to the bottom of the Unending Sea.
