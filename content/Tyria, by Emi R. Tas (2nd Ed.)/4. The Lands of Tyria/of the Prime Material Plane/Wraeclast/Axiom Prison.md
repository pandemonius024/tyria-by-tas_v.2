#ASCENSION 

Axiom prison was a holding facility on southern-mainland [[Wraeclast]]. 

In the ASCENSION campaign, the party of ASCENSION raided Axiom prison after catching wind that an ancient treasure could be sealed within. After scouring the prison's lands, the only ancient thing ASCENSION found was Brutus, the warden of Axiom during its operative period. After making short work of Brutus, ASCENSION uncovered a tie to [[Dialla]], an eccentric necromancer responsible for keeping both Brutus, and Axiom, in a state of undeath.

Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, Axiom prison was sunk to the bottom of the Unending Sea. Curiously, its undead residents continue to exist in their unlife, drowning forevermore.
