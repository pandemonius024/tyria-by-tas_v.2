#ASCENSION 

Sarn was a self-sufficient city in south-central mainland [[Wraeclast]]. Gladiatorial and free, Sarn was unique, free from constant undead harassment and assaults from the insane. Sarn was located south of nearby mountain range, and due north of [[Lioneye's Watch]]. Sarn generated a bit of an economy through gladiatorial spectacles and other theatrics. 

Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, Sarn was summarily sunk to the bottom of the Unending Sea.
