The Vastiri desert was an expansive desert covering [[Wraeclast]]'s north-east-eastern shores. The Vastiri desert stretched from the northeastern shore of mainland Wraeclast, down and westward towards the mountain range north of Sarn.

Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, the Vastiri desert was sunk to the bottom of the Unending Sea.
