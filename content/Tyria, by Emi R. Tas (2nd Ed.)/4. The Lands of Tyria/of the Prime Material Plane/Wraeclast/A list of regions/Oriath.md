Oriath was an isolated island-state off the southeast shore of [[Wraeclast]]. Oriath's capital was [[Theopolis]], where most all of the citizenry of Oriath resided. Oriath was governed by the [[Ebony Legion]], a theocratic authoritarian body who separated the island from external pressures, and isolated its denizens.

Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, Oriath sunk to the bottom of the Unending Sea, where it remains.
