Theopolis was the capital of [[Oriath]], an island-based state off the southeast shores of Wraeclast. Theopolis was ruled under the iron fist of the [[Ebony Legion]], a theocratic authoritarian state devoted to the rites and worship of a sun-lit god named Innocence.

Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, Theopolis was sunk into the Unending Sea.
