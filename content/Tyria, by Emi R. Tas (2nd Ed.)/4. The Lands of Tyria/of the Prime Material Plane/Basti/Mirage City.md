#Dreamwalker 

Murasarab, the City of Magic, the Mirage City. The Mirage City is based entirely on magic, from agriculture to sanitation, with a growing animosity towards travelers due to ongoing events. It may very well be the peak of magic within [[Basti]]. The city runs on its own, and automatically shifts itself away from the sun; it sits on the west side of the Flats during sunrise, and on the east side during sunset. Upon entering the city, a traveler immediately notices a sheer drop in temperature, due to the eternal night that the Mirage City lives within.

Mirage City's citizens believe that Vykem (and therefore, any other traveler,) are the reason for the ongoing haunts and kidnappings. The rumors continue and occasionally, people can hear of "another world" being the source of the problem. 

Also, there's Mega Mash Brothers.
