The story of the Gallows is a legend beyond the fogs of [[Castus, the Fallen Citadel]]. Those who follow the moving lanterns will be lost in the horrid darkness. A land corrupted by the combination of dark magic and Rox's failed experiments. 

The party eliminated an evil Vykem member here, who had gone off the grid, aiming to use all the magic present to resurrect a fallen dragon, and claim its aide.
