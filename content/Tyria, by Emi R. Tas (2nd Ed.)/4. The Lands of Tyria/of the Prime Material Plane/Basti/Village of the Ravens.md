#Dreamwalker 

The Village of the Ravens is a small farming village, with a community of ravens present who are viewed as equals in their society. The village had previously been afflicted by a smattering of Rox's magic, which drove the ravens away, and mimicked the destruction of [[Arakaali]]. 

Bandits had been ordered by a neutral party to help keep the village afloat. The destruction since been cleared by the party, and Vykem has stopped the village from being sacrificed to a god, I'm sure, by Rox's orders. Rox would have soon attempted to retake the village, had he not perished at the hands of the Dreamwalkers.

In the Eclipsed timeline, two refugees from the Village of the Ravens seek aid from the party, who kindly take them in. Two brothers, fleeing on foot from the molten gold raining from the sky.
