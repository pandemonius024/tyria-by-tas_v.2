The Tymfros Range is a mountain in southeastern [[Basti]]. The Tymfros Range begins north of the Village of the Ravens, stretching on its eastward side, all the way down to the Unending Sea. The Tymfros Range contains a singular glade, in which individual members of the Wykin cleansed themselves of Rox' Dream, freed themselves from Rox' influence, and became [[Vykem]].
