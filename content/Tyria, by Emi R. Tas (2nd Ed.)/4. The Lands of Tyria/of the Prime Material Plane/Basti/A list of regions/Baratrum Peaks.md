A mountain range sitting over a trove of mineral and ore. Halflings previously lived in the Forge happily, until the involvement of Rox, who enslaved them with the help of Warforged. Vykem has since liberated them. Aside from the halflings, deeper into the mines are the fiery lizard folk, as well as ancient Celestial ruins. In contrast to the flames below, the peaks are home to freezing blizzards and common snow creatures. Gobbos can be found establishing camps.
