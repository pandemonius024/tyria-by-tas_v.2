Nirvana is the name of the primary residence of [[Cassandra Lumen]]. Nirvana is located in [[Dweomerheart]], and is built around celestial study. Nirvana itself is only loosely guarded, relying on the abject difficulty to navigate into Dweomerheart, and the spellcasting capabilities of its proprietor for primary defense. Its campus is expansive, boasting: two full-sized celestial telescopes, an open-air/open-space observatory, numerous greenhouses (or rather, spacehouses,) fit to sustain celestial plant and animal life, and an inter-planar communicator hooked into the roots of [[Lumen's Blight]], among other amenities.
