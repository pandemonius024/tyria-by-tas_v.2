Abbie is [[Eliza Khione]]'s childhood friend, and SOULREND's former navigator. Abbie left her known life behind in [[Lunti]] to bring SOULREND to Revanon. When the time came to decide who would travel across timelines to a new world, Abbie ultimately decided to stay behind with [[Selene Silvermoon]]. Upon her return to Lunti, Abbie studied shipcraft under Beau, hoping to bring Lunti into a seafaring age.
