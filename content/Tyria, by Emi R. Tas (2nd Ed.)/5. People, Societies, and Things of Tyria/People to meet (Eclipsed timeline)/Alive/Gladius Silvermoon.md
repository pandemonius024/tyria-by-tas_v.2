Gladius Silvermoon is the father of [[Selene Silvermoon]] and [[Aparine Silvermoon]], and widower to [[Rubia Silvermoon]]. Gaius is broadly built, mid-40s, grey-haired, with a cold and commanding presence. His authority often blankets the room before he enters, and he ensures his directives are never disobeyed -- unless your name is Selene. He is the leader-elect of [[Lunti]], and the head of their counsel, the Gawin. Gladius has elevated and clarified the roles of the Gawin and the Tawod, bringing much-needed governance to Lunti when it was disheveled, disorganized, and still reeling from the [[Eclipse]]. 

Prior to the SOULREND campaign, Gladius hired [[Eliza Khione]] to keep an eye over Selene. During the SOULREND campaign, Gladius voiced his opposition to Selene's venture overseas. Gladius was concerned for Selene's safety, not only venturing out beyond the reaches of Lunti, but too venturing into the same circumstances that killed Gladius' wife, [[Rubia Silvermoon]].
