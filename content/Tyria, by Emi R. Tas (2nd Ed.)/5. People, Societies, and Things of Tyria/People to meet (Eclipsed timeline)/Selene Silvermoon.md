Selene Silvermoon is the defiant daughter of [[Gladius Silvermoon]] and [[Rubia Silvermoon]], and sister to [[Aparine Silvermoon]]. Selene, figuring that she wasn't cut out for politique and leaderly life, instead opted to open a hole-in-the-tree clinic. Using her experience (or, lack thereof) in medical practice, Selene offered medical consult for dirt cheap rates.

Gladius, worrying for Selene's safety, but ultimately knowing that he could never reel her in, assigned [[Eliza Khione]] to the protection of his daughter. Gladius offered Eliza a handsome salary in exchange for the observation and protection of Selene, to which Eliza accepted. Selene, aware of the deal, took in Eliza anyway as insurance against any pissed-off customers, angry at Selene's malpractice.

In the SOULREND campaign, Selene decided to leave Lunti following an offer from Mileina. Splitting from Gladius, with Eliza at her side, Selene would follow in her mother's footsteps and sail for Revanon. Unlike her mother, Selene would be successful, and land on an empty Asteria. 

At the bottom of Lunaris' citadel, allegedly on the moon, Selene and company discovered a portal. Right before Selene entered this portal, she dreamed of home and its comforts, and instead directed herself back to Lunti. In doing so, she opened up a portal which so happened to be placed right next to Aparine, sending herself into Lunti, and Aparine into Revanon.

Selene, at Lunti after the expedition was completed, would be placed under house arrest by Gladius for the willful defiance of a Gawin's word. There, in the Silvermoon house, would Selene remain, until Gladius determines that she's finally learned her lesson.
