Lumen's Blight is a small, potted tree pruned into shape with care and precision by [[Cassandra Lumen]]. Its leaves have a deep blue hue, and ingesting the leaves establishes a psychic link between the creature that consumed it, and Lumen herself. The Blight was originally formulated as a means for Lumen's friends and allies to reach her without the use of Sending, or the need to cross planar barriers.

Dante was gifted a pot of Lumen's Blight by Lumen after ETERNITY solved the puzzle within [[Pavo Perilos]]. Dante later used the Blight in a number of situations in order to seek counsel from Lumen.
