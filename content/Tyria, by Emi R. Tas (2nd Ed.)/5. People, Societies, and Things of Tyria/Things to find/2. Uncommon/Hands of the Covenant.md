*Wondrous Item (uncommon)*

A spellcasting focus unique to devotees of the [[Light]]. 

Originally, the *Hands* were leather-bound, rough hewn gloves. The leather would be interlaced with decorative metals and gems, creating a rather fashionable, but restrictive gauntlet. These gloves would then be blessed by priests of the Light, which would imbue them with the necessary aetheric channels for magically inclined wearers to use. The hands are a spellcasting focus which can be worn on one or both hands. Usage of the hands typically requires a free hand, through which the [[Luminiferous Aether]] can be channeled.
