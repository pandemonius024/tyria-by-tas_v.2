#ETERNITY 

Tags: Enchantment, Magical
**Price** 8 gp
**Usage**: Held in 1 hand, as a regular quill; **Bulk** -

*This quill aids users in the recollection and recording of their dreams. The feather stores loose echoes of the users' thoughts as they sleep, which can be accessed by activating this item. The feather stores the dreams of those who dream near it, and return them to you as you wake.* 

**Activate**: 2 action Interact; **Frequency** once per day, following dreams or other sleep-like visions; **Effect**: The feather grants you visions of what it contains, as you slowly become aware of whatever dreams it managed to catch in the previous night. The visions take place in your minds' eye, in much the same way a day dream may take place.
