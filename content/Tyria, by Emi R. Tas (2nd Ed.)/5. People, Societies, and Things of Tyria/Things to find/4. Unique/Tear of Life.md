The Tear of Life is a shard of pure, crystalline [[Luminiferous Aether]], shed by [[Tyria]] while she wept over the bodies of her dead children. The Tear of Life was said to be held by [[Lunaris]] and [[Solaris]] in their eternal celebration of life, their unending duel. After the fall of Hasha, [[Tangmazu, the trickster]] managed to steal the Tear of Life. Tangmazu then used its power to fashion and control an army of clones of the Hashan wizard, Dimitri.

The Tear of Life fell upon the dried crust of one of the Divinarius' corpse, and was buried between the clay and dirt. As legends would have it, grass began to sprout around it, and two blades of grass grew abnormally large. From these two blades of grass, spawned Lunaris and Solaris, sun and moon both, gracing the divine corpses with light, and heat. Lunaris and Solaris would dance about Tyria in rhythm and stride for millennia to come, giving life to the divine corpses.

After SOLSTICE's victory over Tangmazu, Dante du Fleur processed the Tear of Life, the Tear of Time, and the Tear of Space into a singular Tear, dubbed [[Eternity]]. Using Eternity, Dante willed the then-divided Material Plane, Realm of Chaos, and Shadowfell together into the Prime Material Plane in an event known as Solstice. He then threw himself into the Void, along with Eternity, forever divorcing Tyria from this singular source of power.
