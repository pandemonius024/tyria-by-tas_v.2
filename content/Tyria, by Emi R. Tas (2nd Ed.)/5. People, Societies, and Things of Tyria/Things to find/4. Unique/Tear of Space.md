The Tear of Space is a shard of crystalline [[Luminiferous Aether]], shed by [[Tyria]] while she wept over the bodies of her dead children. The Tear of Space was suspended in the floating tower, Pavo Perilos, by the then-patron of knowledge, [[Cassandra Lumen]]. During the events of the ETERNITY campaign, the party come across the Tear of Space, and dislodge it from the hands of the monk carrying it, causing Pavo Perilos to crash to the ground.

The Tear of Space fell past the divine corpses, and into the Void that surrounded Tyria. As it fell, it brought with it an invisible field of Aether, an infinitesimally dense, all-encompassing fabric through which magic began to spread. By observing the velocity of the background magic waves, Cassandra Lumen was able to calculate the center of its propagation, and thus find the Tear of Space.

During the ECLIPSE campaign, Dante used the Tear of Space to suspend gravity around him and the ECLIPSE party, during a fight with a Megaprimatus. The Megaprimatus proved itself to be a threat after killing Graviar, pushing Dante to use the Tear offensively. A 30 ft.-radius column had the effects of gravity undone, causing the giant ape and party to float haphazardly upwards, until the Megaprimatus was at definitely-lethal height, and all fell downwards. Comedically, the corpse of just-killed Graviar floated upwards, too, only to plummet, dead, to the ground with everyone else.

After SOLSTICE's victory over Tangmazu, Dante du Fleur processed the Tear of Life, the Tear of Time, and the Tear of Space into a singular Tear, dubbed [[Eternity]]. Using Eternity, Dante willed the then-divided Material Plane, Realm of Chaos, and Shadowfell together into the Prime Material Plane in an event known as Solstice. He then threw himself into the Void, along with Eternity, forever divorcing Tyria from this singular source of power.
