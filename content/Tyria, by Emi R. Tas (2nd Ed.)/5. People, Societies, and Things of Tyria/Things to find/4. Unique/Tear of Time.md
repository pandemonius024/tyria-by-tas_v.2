The Tear of Time is a shard of crystalline [[Luminiferous Aether]], shed by [[Tyria]] while she wept over the bodies of her dead children. The Tear of Time was summoned to [[King Anton]]'s location in Hasha's throne room, in his desperate bid to save his citizens from the neighboring Brudel. In the beginning of ETERNITY, the Tear of Time would eventually be picked up by one [[Anton Essa]], under direction from Tangmazu, the trickster.

The Tear of Time fell from Tyria's cheek, and through the annals of time into King Anton's hands. Seconds before his death at Tyrus Terim's blade, Soultaker, Time manifested in his hand, and granted him his singular wish for safety for his citizens. The Tear of Time locked Hasha's inner walls in perpetual festivities, her citizens blissfully unaware of the fate that had met them. Past the outer walls, the peasantry and foot soldiers were locked in eternal siege, ghosts made to suffer their cataclysm for eternity.

Following Essa's claiming of the Tear of Time, the ETERNITY campaign party would develop a personal vendetta against the guy, eventually slaying him and taking the Tear for themselves. Both halves of Hasha were freed from the temporal bubble after ETERNITY slew Anton Essa, and took the Tear of Time for themselves. Agnar Lightbringer, as temporary Keeper of the Tear, would later gift it to Nox after learning of his and the rest of the Dreamwalkers' betrayal at Mayhausen Keep.

Nox then used the Tear to reverse the events leading up to the Eclipse. By using the Tear of Time to undo the decision the Dreamwalkers made prior to the Eclipse, Nox (and by extension, Agnar), thrust the world into Tyria's Solstice, the dawn of an age in which the planes were united under a single realm of existence, and the instability wrought by Pharasma, the inevitable and her actions were reversed.

After SOLSTICE's victory over Tangmazu, Dante du Fleur processed the Tear of Life, the Tear of Time, and the Tear of Space into a singular Tear, dubbed [[Eternity]]. Using Eternity, Dante willed the then-divided Material Plane, Realm of Chaos, and Shadowfell together into the Prime Material Plane in an event known as Solstice. He then threw himself into the Void, along with Eternity, forever divorcing Tyria from this singular source of power.
