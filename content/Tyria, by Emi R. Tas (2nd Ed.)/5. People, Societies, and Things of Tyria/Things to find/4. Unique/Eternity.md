Eternity was a relic fashioned by [[Dante du Fleur]] following the SOLSTICE party's victory over Tangmazu, the trickster. Having assembled the [[Tear of Time]], [[Tear of Space]], and [[Tear of Life]], the SOLSTICE party had dominion over the realms of Tyria. Dante, following a vote from the present party, decided to meld the Tears together into Eternity, and use it to mend the then-split planes. He channeled his will through Eternity, reconstituting the Material Plane, the Realm of Chaos, and the Shadowfell into the Prime Material Plane. This event is known as the [[Solstice]].
