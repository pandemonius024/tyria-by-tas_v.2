#SUNDER 

1 is an elusive figure tenuously associated with the [[Unseen]]. Prior to the SUNDER campaign, 1 sent out letters to indebted adventurers, demanding that they come to Baveil to repay their debts. 1 was responsible for extending [[Cradeyr]]'s lifespan, and providing life-saving medicine to [[Ashton]]'s sick parents, among other involvements.
