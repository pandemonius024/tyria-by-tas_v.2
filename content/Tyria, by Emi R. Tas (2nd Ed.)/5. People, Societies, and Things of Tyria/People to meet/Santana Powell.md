#SUNDER 

One of [[Queen Wynmoria]]'s closer friends, Santana is a member of the Queen's court of [[Dawnguard]]. She's a High Elf, hailing from the mountains near [[Elkmire]], and is thus considerably tall. She is a staunch medieval-equivalent-of-globalist, and is caring for the common person. She is cavalier in her speech and actions, but calculated in execution.

Her core motive is the unification of the High Elves and the Humans, and is secondarily moved by displays of magical prowess. Her research into spiritual equality drove her to discover the [[Maxim Celestia]], a set guidelines outlining what qualifies a creature to be Remembered.
