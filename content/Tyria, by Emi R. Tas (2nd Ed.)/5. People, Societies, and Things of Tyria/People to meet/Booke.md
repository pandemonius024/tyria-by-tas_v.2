Booke is one of the elves of [[Tyria's Root]], who was cursed into the form of a book following a terse exchange with a petty wizard. Booke's name was, in fact, Booke, before being turned into a book. 

Following the ASCENSION campaign, Booke set out to Tyria's Root to return home. Upon arriving at Tyria's Root, Booke discovered that an infectious ailment has fallen upon her people, and moved to liberate them. After burning away the webs and fungi that strangled her elves, Booke would remain in Tyria's Root, mingling with her people, and containing an ever-longer history in her pages.

In the Eclipsed timeline, Booke remains undercover within Tyria's Root as minions from the Realm of Chaos invade. Eventually, Booke comes across the party of SOULREND, assisting them somewhat with information and guidance through, and beyond the tree.

In the Solsticed timeline, Booke remains in Tyria's Root, enjoying her life as a leather-bound, self-writing book.
