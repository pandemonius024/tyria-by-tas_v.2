#ASCENSION 

Ice-based archer raised by wolves, now-homesteader with [[Frater Automatus]], Mei is many things, if not calculated. Mei was found by Frater on a military expedition, surrounded by wolves. The wolves seemed to have taken Mei in as one of their own. Frater, pitying Mei, decided to take her in.

Mei lives a free-roaming and unrestrained lifestyle. When Frater was serving in the military as Frater Autonomous-05, Mei kept her distance, only popping in when convenient, and safe. When Frater took his leave of absence from the military, he and Mei would then live in the countryside together.

Mei has since taken up the craft of potion making and fashioning herbal medicines in the countryside. Mei runs an apothecary, and uses her wolven-heightened plant ID to forge medicines no other apothecary could hope to. Alongside fashioning medicines for income, Mei creates a slew of drugs for Frater -- and only Frater, to enjoy.
