B-MO is [[Dante du Fleur]]'s adopted son, and an automaton. B-MO was originally a small battle-bot used in the defense of [[Lex Lutetia]] against invaders and thieves. After ETERNITY bested these bots, Dante decided to keep B-MO's broken-down chassis in his bag. When ETERNITY was caught in a sandstorm, and [[Aux Chord]] subsequently arrived to grant them solace in an oasis, Dante decided to baptize B-MO in the oasis' waters. When B-MO was removed from the waters, he began to beep in response to ETERNITY's speech, and his monitor flickered similarly. Since that moment, B-MO had traveled alongside Dante, learning the ins and outs of the world from ETERNITY, learning how to gather and generate alchemical reagents from Dante, and learning swordplay from [[Agnar Lightbringer]].

At the Solstice, Dante left into the [[Void]], leaving B-MO behind. B-MO returned to [[Amsa]], fending for himself alongside the budding [[Arachnis]]. One day, B-MO crossed paths with the creator and owner of Amsa, another dhampir artificer, [[Pye, the begotten|Pye]]. Pye recognized B-MO from Dante's descriptions, and immediately took a liking to him. Pye offered to keep B-MO in his custody, an offer that B-MO accepted.

He's been gathering a great deal of engineering knowledge from Pye during Pye's maintenance sweeps of Amsa's internals, learning just how large machinery can be. B-MO has since been at Pye's side, continuing to learn and evolve using the spark of life gifted to him by Dante.
