Eliza Khione was a citizen of [[Lunti]], tasked with the protection of [[Selene Silvermoon]] by [[Gladius Silvermoon]]. Eliza upheld this duty by sticking to Selene's side, from the inside of her medical-malpractice clinic, all the way to the shores of Revanon. At the end of the SOULREND campaign, Eliza elected to travel to the Solsticed timeline with [[Silas]] and [[Parce]], leaving her charge Selene, and her longtime friend [[Abbie]], behind.
