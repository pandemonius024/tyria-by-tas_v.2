Qim Chie is a high elf from [[Elkmire]], and following the REAVE campaign, has been hunting down [[Aux Chord]]. This obsession was born, initially, out of a desire to hire Aux Chord as help for a mission; as this endeavor grew long, Qim Chie's desire grew *carnal.* Qim Chie spent years hunting down the globe-trotting hero, only to finally nail him down in the year 652 AS, when Qim Chie cornered Aux Chord in [[Southmoor]].
