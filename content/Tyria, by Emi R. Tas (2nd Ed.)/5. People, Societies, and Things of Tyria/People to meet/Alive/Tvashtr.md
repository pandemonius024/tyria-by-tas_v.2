Tvashtr is a stone dwarf who survived the extinction of the dwarves. He was found cocooned in the depths of [[Mor Proxima]] by ETERNITY, to whom he lent his metalworking skills in thanks. [[Agnar Lightbringer]]'s armor was given a unique trimming to distinguish himself from the [[Soldiers of Strife]].
