Pye is one of the creators of [[Amsa]], and is the prevailing owner of the walking city. He is a dhampir, well-dressed, and tall. He is eloquent and foreboding on first blush, transforming into a soothing presence once you gain his trust. Pye is driven to discover the fundamental mechanisms of the universe; his creation, Amsa, was originally designed towards that end. Pye is a former friend of one [[Cassandra Lumen]], whom he worked with during the creation of Amsa.
