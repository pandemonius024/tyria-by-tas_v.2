Cornelius Xandra was the inventor of the [[Warforged]], autonomous killing tools developed for use by the [[Dawnguard]] military. Cornelius was known to be exceedingly impulsive, and have a caustic personality, making him a difficulty to work with. Cornelius' lab had been destroyed since his death; the relationship between his lab's implosion and his death is uncertain. Cornelius was known to go around wearing only what he claimed was most comfortable for him: a pockmarked, black trench coat, and undergarments.
