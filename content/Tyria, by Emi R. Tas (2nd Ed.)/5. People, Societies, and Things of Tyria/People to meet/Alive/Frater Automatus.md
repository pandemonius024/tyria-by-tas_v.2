Frater Automatus is a retired [[Warforged]], but one of a line of unrepentant killing machines. See: [[LOG - Frater Automatus]] for more details. Frater's form is overwhelming and bulky, designed to be combatively superior over all other metrics. Frater comes equipped with flight-enabled legs and a slew of artillery options, making Frater the ideal war machine.

In the ASCENSION campaign, Frater's focus was the protection of [[Mei]]. The alien urge to protect Mei, rather than kill her, caused Frater's personality to shift slowly from a ruthless killing machine, to a stoic protector.

Following the ASCENSION campaign, Frater chose to live a quiet life alongside Mei in the countryside. Frater now enjoys the solitude that [[Solva]]'s forests offer, and the serenity that Mei's concoctions impart.
