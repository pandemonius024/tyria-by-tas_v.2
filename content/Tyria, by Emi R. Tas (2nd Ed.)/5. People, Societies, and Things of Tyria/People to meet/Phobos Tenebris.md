Phobos Tenebris was [[Deimos Tenebris]]' older brother. After Deimos unintentionally unleashed his powers on [[Hollowfall]], Phobos met a grisly end. Struck by grief, Deimos managed to harness his power to reconstruct a shadowy visage of Phobos, who then continued to travel across Tyria, independently of Deimos.
