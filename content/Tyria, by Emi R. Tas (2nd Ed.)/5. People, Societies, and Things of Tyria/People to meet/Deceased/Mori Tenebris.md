Mori Tenebris was the name of an alternate-universe version of [[Phobos Tenebris]]. Mori hails from a universe where [[Deimos Tenebris]] never unleashed his powers, intentionally or not, on [[Hollowfall]]. As a result, Mori and Deimos' bond never faded, motivating Mori to traverse across timelines to save him. After Deimos was successfully rescued, Mori's contract with [[Pharasma, the inevitable]] had been satisfied, and Mori was sent back to his original universe.
