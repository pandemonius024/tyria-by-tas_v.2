Anton Essa was a greedy, power-hungry Human who was bought and funded by [[Tangmazu, the trickster]] as Dimitri. Dimitri instructed Anton to raid the ruins of [[Hasha]] in search of the [[Tear of Time]]. Tangmazu planned on picking up the Tear of Time after Anton had acquired it. Anton, however, delayed in his contact with Dimitri, wanting instead to enjoy its power for his own gain, resulting in the Tear of Time falling into ETERNITY's hands instead.

Anton often sported gaudy, jeweled heavy armor, a too-big broadsword, and a huge number of hired lackeys at his side. At the ruins of Hasha, Anton was found harassing survivors of the Brudel's siege. These survivors elected to stay and fight for life in these desecrated lands against all odds, and would've found death for it, had ETERNITY not stepped in.

Anton's actions would gain the enmity of the Agency of Time, a cover group formed by Rox also under the instruction of Tangmazu. The Agency would end up hiring the party of ETERNITY to keep tabs on Anton, with the goals of obtaining the Tear should things go awry. However, when the Dreamwalkers of Mayhausen Keep rose and slew Rox, the Agency functionally collapsed, and none under Tangmazu's influence could fight for the Tear of Time after Anton's death.
