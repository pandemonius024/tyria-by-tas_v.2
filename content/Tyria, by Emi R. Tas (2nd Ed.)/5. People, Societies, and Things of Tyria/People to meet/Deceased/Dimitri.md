Dimitri was [[Hasha]]'s arcane advisor, and he provided guidance to the masses on topics concerning: mages' responsibilities, magical regulations, and behavioral guidelines concerning spells. Dimitri was a scrawny, fair-skinned man with shaggy hair, whose mind and body were focused entirely on the honing of his arcane craft.

In life, Dimitri was a close friend of [[Isko Kidar]], and is responsible for leaking Isko's evacuation plan to [[Mania]], Hasha's wartime advisor. This mistake eventually led to the [[Brudel]] accelerating their marching plans, ultimately costing thousands of Hashan lives that could have been evacuated. Dimitri was devasted in the weeks following [[The Day that Hasha Died]], and turned to the same magic that [[Pharasma, the inevitable]] had been seduced by decades prior. In this moment of weakness, Dimitri was taken over by [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], who used Dimitri's body to enact his will upon the Material Plane.
