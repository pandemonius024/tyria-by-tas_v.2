Mileina is a human sorcerer originating from the [[Tower of Magic]], the very same repository of magical prowess that [[Signus]] originated from. When the Tower of Magic suddenly vanished into thin air, Mileina was caught within. Mileina, independently of the Tower of Magic, appeared to the party of ETERNITY in [[Amsa]], from a series of chaotic portals opened topside. After emerging from those chaotic rifts, Mileina immediately recognized Signus, and formed a fixation upon him. Over the course of several days, Mileina stalked Signus, whispering into his ear via arcane messaging, and eventually coerced him into living with her in the [[Realm of Chaos]].

In the ETERNITY campaign, Mileina was available for the party to ask questions concerning the relationship between the planes, and any other arcane-related questions they may have had. The party eventually learned that Mileina was a dominant force within the Realm of Chaos, possessing plane-shaping capabilities and a breadth of knowledge and power in the space. 

In the Eclipsed timeline, Mileina eventually lost Signus to the powers of Chaos, and suffered a tragic decline in mental stability and demeanor. Mileina then resigned herself to being the patron sorcerer of [[Lunti]], a tree-based town in central Wraeclast that lived off of what remains of nature.

In the SOULREND campaign, Mileina tasked the party of SOULREND with the deliverance of a letter to one Agnar Lightbringer. This letter informed Agnar of the truly dire state that the world lived in on the other side of Tyria, and gave him the confidence to part ways with the Tear of Time, and give away what hope remained. After Mileina's letter was delivered, Mileina remained in Lunti, resigned to Signus' loss, and stood at Lunti's side until the bitter end.

In the Solsticed timeline, no such tragedy awaited Mileina. Mileina and Signus continued to live a surprisingly comfortable domestic life in the Realm of Chaos; Mileina taking pleasure from life's simplicity, and Signus continuing to perfect his craft. Once the Solstice merged the planes back together, Mileina's townhome slotted into the other townhomes of Cat Alley. Since then, Mileina and Signus' innumerable cats have been roaming Cat Alley, and the streets of Amsa at large.
