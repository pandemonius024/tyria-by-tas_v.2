#ETERNITY 

Deimos is a half-Drow, half-Elf, sorcerer-warlock agent of [[Unseen]]. He reported directly to [[Ambrose Tenebris]], his adoptive uncle, after Five's brother, his father, died. Five's father, as well as Deimos' brother, [[Phobos Tenebris]], died when Deimos was pushed too far as a child, and summoned forth deadly monsters from beyond. Now, Deimos and Phobos-as-a-shade-reconstruction-of-Phobos live with Five, plotting towards the death of [[Pharasma, the inevitable]].

Once upon a time, Deimos was but a timid boy, playing with his brother Phobos in the comforts of his hometown. One day, Phobos decided to play with a crossbow, pointed it at Deimos, and struck the fear of death into his brother. Stricken with terror, Deimos unwittingly unleashed portals into the Shadowfell, summoning forth vicious monsters from beyond. Phobos, their father, and the rest of their hometown -- massacred in moments. Deimos, alone, survived.

In the ensuing panic, Deimos fled. Finding comfort under shade, Deimos made a desperate plea to the shadows within him to return his brother to him.

They listened.

Tearing his soul into two, Deimos' psyche was split into Deimos and Phobos. Following the events of ETERNITY, Deimos discovered that his time on Tyria was limited, thanks to this fact.

Phobos would sacrifice himself to Deimos at the graves at La Escuela de la Montanha, following direction from Five. That lost piece of Deimos' soul, as Phobos, assured Deimos that it would all be ok, and repaired himself within Deimos soul.

When those touched by the Tear of Time were called upon by Tangmazu, the trickster, Agnar would unwittingly carry Deimos far too close to Tangmazu, resulting in Deimos being torn from Phobos. This left Phobos' psyche within Deimos' body, a mismatch which resulted in Phobos entering a critical condition, for which another brother was summoned to remedy.

[[Mori Tenebris]], an older alternate-reality version of Phobos, appeared at Pharasma's call. Pharasma, beseeching the pleas of a desperate Phobos, pulled Mori's soul from a reality in which the brothers had never fought, and their town never destroyed. 

With Pharasma's summon, Mori traveled with the party of ETERNITY in order to rescue Deimos from Tangmazu, and re-fuse him into his body -- hopefully, with Phobos yet intact.

In the Eclipsed timeline, Mori fails to reconstitute Deimos and Phobos, resulting in the original brothers' becoming too fragile to maintain on Tyria, killing them both. Pharasma, recognizing Mori's plight is futile, silently returns him home.

In the Solsticed timeline, Mori rescues Deimos from Dimitri's labs in the Realm of Chaos. As Mori ultimately crossed Dimitri, Pharasma rejects him to his home, in accordance with her agreement with Dimitri. Deimos would then, ultimately, end up in Pharasma's grasps, and used as her speaker in a taunting manner.

Following the Solstice, Pharasma tasked Deimos with disposing of Phobos' broken soul. Defying her curse and retaining his memories, Deimos instead transports them out of Pharasma's domain using a secret ritual circle that Five taught them early on. Reunited at last, the brothers live with Five, who enjoys an early retirement from the Unseen.
