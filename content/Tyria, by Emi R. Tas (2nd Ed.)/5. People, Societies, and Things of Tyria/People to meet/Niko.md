#BANE 

Niko is an eccentric cave diver in the southern Metrica area. Niko is relatively short, due to his permanent hunch, and appears to be covered in soot and dust at all times. Niko is often found wearing spelunking gear regardless of the environment that he is currently in. His temperament is best described as equal parts easily excitable, and unremittingly curious.

Just before the BANE campaign, Niko invited a number of the BANE members to defend him against potential threats within the [[Maji Pyramid]] off the jungle near [[Tolevia]].
