A jovial tavernkeeper, and owner of the White Rose in [[Asteria]], Barkhum is a cornerstone of Asterian society. He stands at just over 6'2", is roundly built, and sports a hearty laugh that is said to shake the plates of diners around him. Barkhum found his roots in [[Southmoor]], where he ran a similar tavern to the White Rose, on a smaller scale. Barkhum ideates peace, aims to quench disagreements in his customers before they turn into conflict, and wishes nothing more than to keep all his patrons fed. Often, Barkhum will provide meals free of charge to those less fortunate of Asteria. This habit of his has gained him the respect of his community, who will leap to his defense at the slightest threat to his livelihood. Barkhum is a cornerstone of Asterian society, and shows no sign of slowing down.
