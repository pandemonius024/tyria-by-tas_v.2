Anessa Malgasi Eupheria, or Princess Eupheria, was the monarch-to-be of [[Mayhausen Keep]], prior to the Dreamwalkers' dismantling of the false king. Eupheria lost her princess status following the collapse of the Keep's structure of governance, and after [[Rox]]' insurgent actions were revealed to wider Basti, Eupheria was forced into anonymity. 

In the Eclipsed timeline, Princess Eupheria was caught by the barrage of glacial moonlight cages fired by Lunaris. Later, she would be brought into the Keep's prison, along with all other frozen citizens. There, she remained.

In the Solsticed timeline, Princess Eupheria continued to hide amongst citizens of the Keep. She has no plans on resuming the monarchy as it was, and intends on merely living out her life, separate from her courtly past.
