#ASCENSION #ETERNITY 

Des is a dark red skinned Tiefling bard, with ram horns and typically slutty attire. Des was slain following the ending of ASCENSION, after [[Jaemji Wanwon the fifth]] pulled the Fiend from the Deck, causing everyone to be hunted down for sport. Like Jaemji, Des was cut down, and his soul torn apart across the afterlives.

In the Eclipsed timeline, Dante du Fleur and Qatar Altan (and Bones' unobserving skeleton) find Des in [[Calistria]], lounging just outside the city with a friend. Indiana Bones (the observing skull) and Mori Tenebris find Des in [[Alethia]], who was ferried alongside them down the river Acheron. Lastly, Agnar and Iron Fist Alexander found Des in the [[Boneyard]], awaiting judgement evermore.
