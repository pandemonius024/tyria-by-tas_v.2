#ETERNITY 

Auntie was a half-Goliath, half-Human leader of the now-defunct [[Weave]]. Auntie was born in the frigid northern realm of [[Sa'en Mawqi]], where she gained much of her ferocity and brutish strength. Wishing for a better life, Auntie would join a caravan that made rounds about Revanon, eventually dropping off at Asteria. There, Auntie heard rumors of a gambling haven in [[Amsa]], and made for Amsa to make a living for her and her family as muscle for casinos. In Amsa, Auntie worked her way into infamy as an armed guard of Amsan casinos, gaining a reputation as being brutally capable, with a keen eye for troublemakers.

Things would turn south rapidly following her first year as hired muscle. She would at once, learn of her family's death in the northern realms, and a threat to her current employers: a religious sect named Eternos. Eternos, damning gambling as a sinful practice, identified Auntie as one of many "symptoms of a sickness" in Amsa, and began to harass her -- and her employer. The casino for which she worked to defend, cut her off the moment things got sour.

Auntie, alone and desperate, turned to Amsa's underside for a coin and dinner. She would learn, through the course of surfing friend's bunks, that Eternos was vying for power, and using religious cleanliness as a front for their scheme. Their claims of blood libel, rapist Goliaths, Elven supremacy, and the need for spiritual cleanliness rang hollow, at the sight of their mustering for war in Amsa's chassis. 

Auntie would not let Eternos come to power so easily. She began to muster for war herself, gathering the likes of [[Virgil]], and [[Dante du Fleur]]; securing a reclusive, abandoned wreck in Amsa for her and her new family; taking favors and gaining the good fortune of Amsa's underside. Well into her halfway-years, Auntie labored tirelessly to safeguard her new companions, and protect Amsans from a greater threat than they could imagine.

In the course of ETERNITY, Auntie would become comrades-in-arms with the party of ETERNITY, gaining a particular respect for Agnar Lightbringer's strength. She would teasingly force food down all their gullets, with Dante playing second fiddle. Dante would pretend that Auntie would be severely offended by their inability to finish their food, while Auntie would play up the passive-aggression to pressure them. In this way, Qatar in particular would earn Auntie's respect, by eating until sickness to appease her.

In the Eclipsed timeline, Auntie disappears mysteriously following a lead on Eternos' bargaining with Pye, the begotten. In their conversing, Auntie learned of a conspiracy involving Elven weaponry, and would leave behind the note "...what of the Elves?"

In the Solsticed timeline, Auntie would be slain by the hands of Damian Ashborne's mind-controlled Soldiers of Strife, in a joint raid with Eternos themselves. Agnar, foreseeing the siege in their absence, gave instructions on how to best fortify their encampment. In ETERNITY's absence, Eternos sieged the Weave's safehouse. Over the course of several days, Auntie led the efforts to hold fast against the siege. One day before Auntie estimated their entrances would give way, she gave Virgil the instruction to call for Dante's aid. Dante, having just been scalded by Auntie's consideration of selling them out, sent only Manetto Donati. Manetto was not nearly enough aid.

The Weave were summarily routed once Eternos broke through. Auntie's life work would be eradicated after a days' long siege, but not before she took down a good handful of Soldiers herself. Auntie would fight for hours after the walls came down, shielding as many of her Weave as possible. She was pushed back into her room by the Soldiers, where she fought until her end.
