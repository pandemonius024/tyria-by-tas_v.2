Iron Fist Alexander was [[Dante du Fleur]]'s companion and aide, remaining dutifully at his side until the very end. Alexander was an iron-cast cauldron with iron legs and arms; no face, but a resounding voice. Alexander seemed to be able to see and hear, as he would follow at Dante's side without the need for guides nor instruction. Alexander found use in his life as a mixing pot, stirring together any dishes, stews, potions, or brews that Dante needed in bulk. 

At his end, Alexander served to concoct the most powerful artifact that Tyria would ever see: the combination of the Tears of Tyria, into a single jewel, [[Eternity]]. The formation of Eternity in his cistern destroyed Alexander. While his iron hide may be gone from Tyria, his barks of "my lord!" echo on in the hearts of the warriors he was so honored to travel beside.
