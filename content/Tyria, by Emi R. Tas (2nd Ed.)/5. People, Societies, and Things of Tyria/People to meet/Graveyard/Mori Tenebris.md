Mori Tenebris is an alternate-universe version of [[Phobos Tenebris]]. Mori hails from a universe where [[Deimos Tenebris]] never unleashed his powers, intentionally or not, on [[Hollowfall]]. As a result, Mori and Deimos' bond never faded, motivating Mori to traverse across timelines to save him.
