King Radovan was the ruler of [[Asteria]] and her realms. As king, Radovan incorporated the unincorporated villages of [[Metrica Province]] into Revanon's domain, quashed anti-elf sentiments amongst separatists, and grew Asteria into a global nexus of trade, arts, and culture.
