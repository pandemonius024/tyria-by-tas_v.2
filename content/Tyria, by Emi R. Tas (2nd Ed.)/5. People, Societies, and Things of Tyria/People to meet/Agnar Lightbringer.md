#ETERNITY 

Agnar Lightbringer was Joey's PC for the ETERNITY saga. He's a 6+ ft., heavily armored ex-[[Soldiers of Strife]], who wears a custom-forged variant of their armor from [[Tvashtr]]. Agnar wields the [[Blade of Eternity]], a weapon originally forged for [[Tyrus, the warbringer]] prior to the Brudel siege of Hasha. Agnar is an honor-bound Oathbreaker, whose internal moral compass stands firm over all other influences. Agnar gained a reputation on Tyria for being a slayer of false gods, and a filter for any daring to reach for divinity.

Agnar was born into the Soldiers of Strife, raised in the harsh climate of the [[Vardousia Range]]. After learning of his ex-wife's infidelity, Agnar challenged the homewrecker, [[Damian Ashborne]] to a duel. The morning of the duel, Agnar prayed to the Soldier's patron, Tyrus for his blessing in his coming duel. Tyrus granted Agnar a blessing that amounted to a good night's sleep, failing to give Agnar the might he needed, and ultimately Agnar lost the duel. Ashamed, Agnar broke his oath to the God of War, and rode off on his journey of revenge. 

Agnar would, following the events of the ETERNITY campaign, become the Keeper of Time. Wielding the Timesplitter, Agnar unknowingly harnessed the powers fashioned by Tyrus, and eventually proved himself more capable a warrior than the God of War himself. 

Agnar tends to default to violence if things don't go his way. Using his overbearing stature, immense might, and naturally intimidating persona, Agnar reshapes the world around him into one of his wishes. 

Notes:
- Soldiers of Strife would dissolve, leaving room for Agnar's Eternal Blades, a brotherhood-at-arms intended to be the spiritual successor to the Soldiers.
