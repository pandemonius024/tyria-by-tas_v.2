Eternos is a religious conclave headquartered in the underside of [[Amsa]]. Eternos is loosely based off of the beliefs and teachings of the [[Eternal Empire]], with some Eternos members believing themselves to be direct descendants of the last Azmeri ruler of the Empire. Eternos is one of the largest organizations commanding the underside of Amsa.
## History
Prior to the ETERNITY campaign, Eternos worked together with [[Tangmazu, the trickster]] to deliver Tangmazu some "interesting specimen". They delivered [[Virgil]] and [[Dante du Fleur]] from the [[Weave]].

During the ETERNITY campaign, Eternos laid low and kept tabs on ETERNITY after they freed Dante, mustering their forces for a lethal strike on the Weave. Towards the end of the ETERNITY campaign, Eternos launched their raid of the Weave's headquarters in tandem with the then-corrupt [[Soldiers of Strife]]. This resulted in the Weave's destruction.

Shortly thereafter, Eternos was met with retribution by none other than the once-captive, Dante du Fleur. Dante, after learning about the Weave's destruction, bombed the entire section of Amsa that contained Eternos' central leadership. 

After the dust had settled, both organizations were nothing more than shadows of their past.

Following the Solstice was a decades-long reconstitution effort on the behalf of Eternos. Eternos' messaging shifted from seeking dominance over Amsa's underside, to projecting the everlasting will of the Eternal Empire. The remaining members of Eternos claimed that their survival was part and parcel of their direct lineage to the last Azmeri rulers, and was thus foretold by the prophets of the Empire. Their story attracted the disparate; their might attracted the desperate.

Eternos and the Weave's successor, [[Arachnis]], have continued to butt heads. Their fight stands unfettered by lower organizations, who have either been squashed by, or incorporated into either of the rival gang's numbers.
