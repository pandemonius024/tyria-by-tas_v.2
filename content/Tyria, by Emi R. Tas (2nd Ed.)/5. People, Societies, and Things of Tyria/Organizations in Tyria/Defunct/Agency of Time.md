The Agency of Time was a small organization formed by [[Rox]], under suspicion of [[Anton Essa]]'s movements concerning the [[Tear of Time]]. Rox, who believed that Anton was moving to keep the Tear rather than deliver it to [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], developed resources and stratagem to track down Anton, and the Tear of Time.

In the ETERNITY campaign, the party are recruited into the Agency of Time after Anton escaped from the party with the Tear of Time. Rox made contact with the group while they were in the annals of a collapsing [[Hasha]], bidding them to find him in [[Amsa]]. In its existence, the Agency gave the party leading information in tracking down Anton.
