The Shattered wasteland is a demi-plane created following [[Pharasma, the inevitable]]'s [[Shattering]] of the [[Prime Material Plane]]. The space between the [[Material Plane]], [[Realm of Chaos]], and [[Shadowfell]] became known as the Shattered wasteland. Tyria, cradled into a ball, was found here. In the time between Pharasma's destruction and Dante's construction, Tyria was the lone force holding the 3 planes together. It is here that Tangmazu, the trickster intended to claim dominion over the planes of Tyria, and here that Dante du Fleur would instead heal the wounds inflicted so long ago. 

Travel between any plane, and the Shattered wasteland requires a portal fashioned out of materials close to E'Lenora at the time of its shattering. Tangmazu would find a way into the wasteland by accessing it directly under the stone pillar used to break the planes in the first place. Isko Kidar fashioned an entryway into the wasteland using remnants of Pavo Perilos, which was once present near E'Lenora.

The Shattered wasteland also fostered a unique relationship with the stability of the gods in Calistria. As Selûne, the enervate's strength diminished, the Shattered wasteland became frigid and dark, and a tundra spread across the space between the planes. This frost threatened the health of Tyria in the long term, and eventually caused Isko Kidar to encourage his son, Qatar Altan, to succeed Selûne.

Following the near-fall of Hasha at the hands of the Brudel, Tyrus left his post in the Brudel military to come to the Shattered wasteland. Tyrus, having witnessed the futures to come during the formation of the Tear of Time, would stay in the wasteland to await the arrival of a suitable leader of the Soldiers of Strife. It was in Agnar Lightbringer, that he found that leader. Having witnessed Agnar triumph over the only remaining candidate, Damian Ashborne, Tyrus silently willed dominion of the Soldiers of Strife to Agnar.
