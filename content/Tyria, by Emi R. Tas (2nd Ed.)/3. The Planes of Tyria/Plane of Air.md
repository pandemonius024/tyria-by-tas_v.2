*via PF2e GM Core pg. 175*

The Plane of Air is a vast realm of wind, storms, and skies. Illuminated by great artificial globes of flame and distant starlight from the Material, the plane is populated by air elementals, dragons, air scamps, and a great empire of genies. Though mostly clouds and empty skies, the plane isn't entirely bereft of solid ground, including rock and ice created by the residents or dragged into their realm from the distant [[Plane of Earth]] or neighboring [[Plane of Water]].
