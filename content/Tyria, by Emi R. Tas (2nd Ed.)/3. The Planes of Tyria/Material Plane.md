One of the few through lines between every campaign on Tyria. The Material Plane is the name of the lands created by the [[Divinarius]], a loose collection of dried clay atop Tyria's belly. The Material is, in comparison planes like the Plane of Flame, the Void, and the Border Ethereal, hospitable to life, and stable enough to sustain generations upon generations of society. It is the prosaic universe, but deeply alone in that regard, as no worlds exist beyond the loose veil around Tyria.

The Material Plane, known after the Solstice as the [[Prime Material Plane]], is the home for pre-incarnate souls, where mortal life is born, lives, and dies, before being sent to the Boneyard for judgement and assignment into Celestia and Alethia.
## Solstice and the Material Plane
The Material is ultimately merged back together with the Realm of Chaos and the Shadowfell following SOLSTICE's decision to meld them all back together. This reformation has resulted in a Prime Material that is topologically identical to the Material (save, for the flooding of Wraeclast), but are scale degrees more violent, and more spontaneous.
