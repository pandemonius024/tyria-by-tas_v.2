The Prime Material Plane is the name of the plane that encircles [[Tyria]]. The Prime Material Plane was created either right before Tyria's first appearance, or immediately following the death of the Divinarius. The Prime Material, originally, was merely a pocket of [[Luminiferous Aether]] surrounding Tyria and her children, containing a few hills, a lake, and a wooden cabin. Over time, it expanded to hold space for the kingdoms and satellites that now exist within it. As the Prime Material grew, it kept stable more space for earth, water, and air, among all other aspects of the concentric planes. 

Following the [[Shattering]], the Prime Material Plane ceased to exist, its place taken by the [[Shattered wasteland]], an aspect of the Void that grew slowly, as the Prime once had. Following the [[Solstice]], the Prime Material is reborn, the Shattered wasteland erased, and the face of Tyria changed evermore.
## Cosmology
The Prime Material Plane is centered upon the Void, a singularity of space and time around which all other planes exist. While it is agreed upon that all planes of the universe are centered upon the Void, its tangible, locatable position has yet to be determined; as such, the Prime Material Plane does not have a tangible center.

The Prime Material is surrounded by the Inner Sphere Planes, known as the home of mortal life, and focus of divine attention. Surrounding the Inner Sphere, as well as the Prime Material, are the Outer Sphere Planes, also known as the manifest realms of philosophy.
## Effects of the Solstice on the Prime Material
In the year 0 AS/BS, the Shattering occurred, dividing the Prime Material into 3 even parts. For centuries the planes remained separate, until the year 633 when the joint parties of SOLSTICE and Dreamwalker initiated the Solstice. At the end of SOLSTICE, Dante channeled Eternity, the sum of the Tears of Tyria, to make 3, 1 once more. Once his work was done, he banished himself and Eternity into the Void, so that none are be tempted by their destructive powers. Not all has been peaceful in this recombination. 

The Shadowfell, having been split into Tyria's intestines, had been tainted with the fray refuse of Tyria. It became infested with corrupt, violent creatures, who craved nothing but the destruction of all they came across. Mania, in defiance, created Axis, who now resides in place of E'Lenora.

Additionally, locations across the Prime Material, henceforth referred to simply as the Material, became "Shadow-touched". These are areas tainted, in part or wholly, by the dark of the Shadowfell.

The Realm of Chaos, having been split into the space around Tyria, had become virulent and uncontrollable. It began to swirl with ancient energies, giving shape to pre-Tyriannic powers, and stirring an ancient horror.

Residences, buildings, laboratories, floating pyramids, and ominous fight-boxes have been placed in and across Tyria as geographically-fittingly as possible. Mileina and Signus' residence has been inserted into Amsa; the Tower of Magic has mysteriously reappeared in Metrica Province.

Additionally, the animals of Tyria began to experience rapid progress evolutionarily, as the essence of Chaos infused within their genes. It became widespread, but uncommon enough to be exciting to the commoner, to find hybrids of animal species, as well as strange, new species. If an animal wasn't merged across orders with another genus, it may have had intellectual changes to make them deadlier, gain limited capabilities with tools, or may have experienced no change at all.

- Pig-bison began to be ranched near Dawnguard for their uniquely meaty taste.
