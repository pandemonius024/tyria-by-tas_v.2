The Remembered is the name of the non-godlike creatures inhabiting [[Celestia]]. The Remembered can be particularly significant species' of animals or individually influential creatures; influential writers, artists, and leaders that have impacted the world, positively or negatively. The Remembered are brought into Celestia following their death, whether that death is a physical one of blood and life, or a symbolic one of crumbling decay. 

The allocation of what or whom to be Remembered is defined in the [[Maxim Celestia]]. As spiritual scholar Santana Powell of Dawnguard outlines it, the Maxim places a "distinct focus on the strength of spirit, presence of mind, and mental impact one has had on other creatures". In general, if a creature or object has become associated with a powerful, unifying emotional response in the creatures around it, it increases its chances of being Remembered.
