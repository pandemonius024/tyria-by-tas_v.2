The Forgotten are the shades within [[Alethia]], which are created after a fallen creature drinks from the river Lethe. Drinking from the river Lethe causes the creature to be washed of their memories, and reduced into a simple flame, bearing the barest amount of their personality. The Forgotten are made to wander about Alethia for an untold amount of time, before they are naturally melded back into the [[Luminiferous Aether]]. In this way, the Forgotten are an intermediary step in a single creature's path to resurrection, or an untold, different purpose.

In the Eclipsed timeline, Laura Croft, Indiana Bones' friend, becomes a Forgotten, and is mixed into the stew of souls flowing through. Bones and company discover Laura Croft after they are split across the planes, and after Croft is killed in a rogue archaeological dig near Tolevia. Bones decided not to approach Croft, who later drinks from the river Lethe.
