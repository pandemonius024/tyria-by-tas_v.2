Axiomites are a [[Warforged]]-adjacent race of pseudo-sentient automatons created by [[Axis]]' ruler, [[Mania]]. The axiomites were created by Mania in direct response to Cornelius Xandra's creation of the warforged, but have fallen short in showcasing truly sentient behavior. This shortcoming has pushed Mania to enslave the elves of [[E'Lenora]], use them as remote pilots for her axiomites, and aid her in furthering her research into creating true sentience.

Axiomites, in their design, take a lot of inspiration from the warforged of Solva. They are mostly humanoid, but not necessarily, and are intended to sport a wide range of sentient capabilities. Larger, humanoid axiomites were intended to be truly sentient, while smaller, automaton drone-like axiomites were only intended to be capable of interpreting and obeying direct orders. Mania has been more successful with her pursuits of the latter, but have fallen short of the former. Production issues, a general misunderstanding of bastilite's properties, and a misguided sense of godliness have all impeded Mania's manic pursuit of the creation of life, and hampered the growth of the axiomites.
