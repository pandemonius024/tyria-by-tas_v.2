Torentia is the god of water, and is as ancient as the very lands upon Tyria. Torentia first appeared upon Tyria immediately following the death of the Divinarius, flooding the cracks in their clay, and saturating the tops of their corpses. It is believed, but not confirmed, that Torentia originates from the [[Plane of Water]], having been invited into the Prime Material by Tyria's will. Torentia has a physical form, but is rarely seen in that form, as he prefers remaining in the [[Unending Sea]]. Torentia's form while moving through the Unending Sea is that of water, rendering him virtually indistinguishable from his surroundings.

Following the rise of [[Kitava, the insatiable]] on Wraeclast's lands, Torentia made the impossible decision to destroy Kitava for good, and sacrifice Wraeclast and all the life upon it. Torentia severed Wraeclast from greater Tyria, by shredding the rock and stone that formed Wraeclast's foundation into mud. The mainland of Wraeclast, and the island of Oriath, were subsumed into the Unending Sea alongside that cannibalistic god of corruption.
