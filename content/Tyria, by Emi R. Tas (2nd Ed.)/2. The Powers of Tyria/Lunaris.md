[[Solaris]]

Lunaris was worshipped deeply, and separately from her sister in [[Mayhausen Keep]], in which a cathedral and its masses, stands. In Dreamwalker, Lunaris emboldens [[Nox]] as her champion, in the aims of gaining enough power to triumph over Solaris in their eternal duel.

In the Eclipsed timeline, Solaris falls to Tyria in a heap of molten gold, as Lunaris laughs over her, and subsequently gloats about it. After a brief period, Lunaris ends up dying at the hands of ETERNITY, with [[Agnar Lightbringer]] claiming her head as a trophy.

Later, [[Eliza Khione]] and her mother would individually come across Lunaris' Moonstone, granting them each aqueous abilities, and Plane-scaping capabilities. Lunaris' voice would speak to its wielder, typically giving advice in a somewhat-bothered manner, as if all this was beneath her and her woes.

In the Solsticed timeline, the lie that [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], spread about the Dancing Sisters collapsed. Mania put forth statements and evidence that dispelled the centuries-old myth about the nature of the Light's inheritance, resulting in the fervent worship of either Lunaris or Solaris diminishing.
