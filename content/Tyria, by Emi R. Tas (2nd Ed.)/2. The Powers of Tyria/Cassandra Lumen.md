Cassandra Lumen is the de facto god of knowledge, patron of academia, owner and proprietor of [[Nirvana]], and director emeritus of [[MIST]]. Lumen is credited for the discovery of [[Dweomerheart]] and the [[Tear of Space]] at its heart. As a long-time denizen of Dweomerheart, Lumen is often found peering into the universe's endless sea of stars, assisting in negotiations for new MIST entries, or otherwise lending her knowledge to the likes of Adrian and Emi R. Tas.

In the ETERNITY campaign, Lumen made contact with the party while they were exploring through her tea room, Pavo Perilos. There, she awarded the party with a means to communicate with her -- a sapling from Lumen's Blight.

After the party pillaged the Tear of Space from Pavo Perilos and sent the floating island tumbling into the Vinovian sand, Lumen merely mourned the collapsed tea room, and moved on.
