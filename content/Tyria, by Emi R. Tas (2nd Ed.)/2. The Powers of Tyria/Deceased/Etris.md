#ETERNITY 

Etris was the god of wind, worshipped primarily by Asterian traders sailing across the Unending Sea, and often placated by the people [[Sa'en Mawqi]]. Etris took the form of a collection of clouds and breezes stretching as tall as 1000 meters. In this cloud form, Etris is centered on their heart, a physical, beating heart that blows wind through it endlessly.

[[Pye, the begotten]], after encountering Etris, slew Etris, and claimed their heart for himself. Following this, Pye installed the heart of Etris into [[Amsa]]'s machinery, enabling hydraulics on a large scale.
