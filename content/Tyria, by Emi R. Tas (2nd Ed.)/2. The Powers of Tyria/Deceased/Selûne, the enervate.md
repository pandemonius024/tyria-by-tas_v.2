 #ETERNITY #WIP 

Selûne was the god of the [[Light]], a herald that watched over Tyria and her creations. Selûne commanded dominion over the forces of Good and Law that streamed in from the [[Outer Sphere Planes]]. Selûne lived in [[Celestia]], enacting her will upon Tyria through visions and scrying. In life, Selûne was often found sporting pale white robes, silver adornments, and carrying herself with weightlessness and brevity. At the end of the SOLSTICE campaign, Selûne met her end with grace when [[Qatar Altan]] ascended to Celestia to take his place as destined heir of the Light.
