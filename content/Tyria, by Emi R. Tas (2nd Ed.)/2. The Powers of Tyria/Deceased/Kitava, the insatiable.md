Kitava is a creature whose sole aim is to consume all life. Kitava came into existence in opposition to the unyielding hopefulness that [[Aris, the unwritten]] has come to embody. Kitava's hunger grows in direct proportion to the scale of life on Tyria, and as such, grew to become a greater and greater threat as society did. Soon after the [[Solstice]], [[Torentia]] deemed it a worthy trade-off to sink the continent of [[Wraeclast]] deep into the water, leaving Kitava to drink from the endless spigot that is the [[Plane of Water]]. 

In the Eclipsed timeline, Kitava is never sunk into the ocean, and is left to consume life on Wraeclast with wanton disregard. As the amount of life on Tyria diminished, so too did Kitava's power.
