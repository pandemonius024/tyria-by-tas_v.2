Tyria is a homebrew world that I've been using as a backdrop for heroic fantasy since ~2019! Over the years I've gathered bits and pieces of lore from my players' backstories and decisions, the media I've been digesting at the time, and all sorts of other sources of inspo.
## Getting started
To the left of your browser of choice, is a search bar and a dropdown menu. If you know what document you're looking for -- great! If you don't, you can try to find it using the dropdown links provided; alternatively, you can try searching for a document based on what campaign it was related to in (e.g. "\#BANE"). In general, I'll tag something with a campaign if a session was spent involving the person, place, or thing.

If you'd like to see a map of the world, [here's my depiction of Tyria!](https://imgur.com/a/MUABCHk) Courtesy of Inkarnate. (last updated: 06-10-2025)
