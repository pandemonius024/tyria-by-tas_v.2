Cor Scant is a humble Asterian village that came under the dominion of [[Eliza Khione]]. Cor Scant had a population of no more than 50 villagers at any one time, all of whom enjoyed the quiet life that the village offered -- that is, until Eliza arrived. Late in the year 633 AS, Eliza descended from the [[Vardousia Range]], and immediately took control over Cor Scant.
