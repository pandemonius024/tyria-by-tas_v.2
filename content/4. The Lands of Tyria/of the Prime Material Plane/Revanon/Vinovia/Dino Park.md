Dino Park is a crater in the mid-western reaches of [[Vinovia]]. Originally formed as a result of falling shelling debris from Hasha's siege, Dino Park gained its titular feature once the [[Tear of Time]] was abused by Anton in the area. Anton, enjoying his powers, and particularly desiring to see his men be chased by velociraptors, blasted the crater millennia into the past. Unbeknownst to Anton, this effect would become permanent, and allowed for dinosaur civilization to form following Anton's departure.

When the party of ETERNITY came around, Qatar Altan and Signus spoke with the locals, and learned of their plight. After helping them form an authoritarian regime, the party departed. From that visitation forward, the denizens of Dino Park would be ruled by the the might of the T-Rex Flametalon, by the side of the velociraptor Arcarus.

There is also a brachiosaurus named Reginald.
