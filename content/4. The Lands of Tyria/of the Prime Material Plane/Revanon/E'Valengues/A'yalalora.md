A'yalalora (Ai-ya-la-lo-ra) is an elven settlement with a population half that of F'mdell. It is located in the mid-eastern marsh forest of [[E'Valengues]], due north of the alluvial deposit created by the local river system. A'yalalora is a quieter, and less-frequently-visited town as compared to other elven settlements, relying mostly on elven survival instincts and wit to persevere. 

Following the Solstice, a small refugee party of E'Lenorans fled to A'yalalora. This influx of shocked and panicked elves threw the quiet town of A'yalalora into unrest, and a growing concern for their long-term safety seeded.
