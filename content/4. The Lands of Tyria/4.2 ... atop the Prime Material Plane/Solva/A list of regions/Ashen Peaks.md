The Ashen Peaks are an seemingly-permanently snow-capped range of mountains that cut [[Solva]] in half. Upon the Ashen Peaks sits Elkmire, an expansive fortress of the high elves. A number of guard towers stretches across the Ashen Peaks, coordinated by smoke signals, and founded on the defense of the Peaks against Dawnguard. The Ashen Peaks house rich, fortuitous mines exposed by the creation of these mountains, making them the target of extended mineral wars from Dawnguard and Elkmire alike.
