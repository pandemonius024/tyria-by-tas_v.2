Icehaven was a humble village on the northern corner of [[Metrica Province]]. It sat in-between the foot of the [[Vardousia Range]], and the lip of the Weeping Lake.
## History
In the year 633 AS, Icehaven was swept away by a cold flame, summoned forth by an unknown force. Hair was singed from corpses, wood and grass was scorched away, yet no burns, no ash, no soot settled from the flames. This devastation was discovered by traders from [[Southmoor]] weeks after the fire had raged. Soon after, Asterian soldiers cleared the ruins and dug a mass grave for the dead. 

In the year 673 AS another unknown force dug up Icehaven's mass grave, and animated a number of the corpses. One of those animated corpses is the revenant [[Qiramisu]]; another is the revenant [[Grème Brûlée]]
