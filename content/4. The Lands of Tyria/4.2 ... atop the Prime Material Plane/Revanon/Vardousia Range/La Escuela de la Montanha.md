La Escuela de la Montanha is an institution for magical study, oriented around the growth and development of a wizard's capabilities. La Escuela is built high into the [[Vardousia Range]], where it is believed that the high air whispers arcane truths that wizards are able to study, and divine magic from. La Escuela was a complimentary institution to the former school, the [[Tower of Magic]]; la Escuela focuses on magic gained from study, while the Tower focused on refining magic that was already present.

Following the ETERNITY campaign, la Escuela scholar [[Erdogan Eilric]] discovered Phobos in a sicky state in Kilhan, and brought him to la Escuela in hopes that they had the resources to help him. After word spread of Phobos' condition, [[Ambrose Tenebris]] caught word, and brought Deimos Tenebris to la Escuela. At la Escuela's graveyard, Ambrose guided Deimos through the process of merging Phobos back into his soul, ensuring Deimos' lasting vitality, but destroying Phobos' physical form. Cradled on the Escuela's shadow crossing, Deimos was successful at melding Phobos back into his soul.
