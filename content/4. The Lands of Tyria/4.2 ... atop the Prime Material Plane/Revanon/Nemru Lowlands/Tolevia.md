Tolevia is a swamp-laden village on the south-western reaches of the [[Nemru Lowlands]], and the starting point of the BANE campaign. Tolevia is often harassed by the denizens of the swamp and neighboring jungle, as well as marauding bandits of elven or human origin. Tolevia, thanks to its distance from Asteria, finds itself largely isolated and left to its own devices by the state, save for the occasional tax man.
