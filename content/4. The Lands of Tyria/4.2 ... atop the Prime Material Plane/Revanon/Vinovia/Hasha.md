Hasha was a city state in the north-western reaches of [[Vinovia]]. It was created in 244 BS when travelers braving the desert banded together, and settled on an abundant oasis. The name "Hasha" comes from the Vinovian word *hasha*, which is an interjection loosely meaning, "stop now", or "that's enough". Hasha survived from its founding in 244 BS, until [[The Day that Hasha Died]] in Eterni of 12 AS when [[Brudel]] breached Hasha.
## History
Immediately following [[King Anton]]'s death wail, Hasha was blasted out of time. The city inside its walls was suspended in a never-ending festival, in celebration of the waxing winter sun. The city outside its walls was suspended in a never-ending siege.
