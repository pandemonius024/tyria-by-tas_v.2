Amsa is a mechanical city whose 6 mechanical legs take it across [[Vinovia]]. It is surrounded, at all times, by a mirage which renders the city invisible to onlookers more than a half mile away. Compasses that have been specifically tuned to Amsa's aetheric signature can be used to navigate to Amsa from beyond this mirage. Amsan compasses are tuned to the aetheric signature of the heart of [[Etris]], a source of raw magic harvested from the ex-god of wind. Ferry services exist across Vinovia, operating most notably in Agrizi and Valentia, who offer safe passage into Amsa at a cost. These ferrymen are often operated by the casinos, cartels, weapons' dealers, and any other group that has a vested interest in pulling more bodies into Amsa. Using the heart of Etris, Amsa's sole remaining creator, [[Pye, the begotten]], can navigate the sands at will.
## History of construction
Originally constructed by the patron of knowledge, [[Cassandra Lumen]], an enigmatic Dhampir artificer, Pye, the begotten, and ex-anthropologist Aviria, Amsa was a walking hovel used as base-camp for the team's surveying. Amsa initially grew famous as an oasis that would spontaneously appear to those lost in the desert, and stories of the gracious engineers who operated it spread like sand on wind. As interest in this walking oasis grew, more types of folk came to see it: artificers interested in the machinery; desperate folk searching for safe harbor; archaeologists looking to join the fray. Some would dedicate themselves to the cause, while others remained by necessity.

As the population grew, demand for space grew, too. The small camp was becoming saturated with sightseers and refugees, and its legs struggled to carry them all. The team's attention turned towards the expansion of Amsa. Pye fashioned an engine that could harness the winds pushed by Etris' still-beating heart, making use of hydraulic systems to power all of Amsa. Aviria made use of the greatly increased mechanical power, and expanded the number of legs from 3 to 6, and its base plate from ~0.5 acres to ~200 acres. Lumen, all the while, was immediately disenchanted with this pursuit, and left the engineers for her residence in Dweomerheart.
## Structure and organization
Amsa is a 200+ acre-footprint machine that hobbles on 6 legs. The legs are divided into 2 groups of 3 on either side of Amsa, and produces a relatively stable gait by alternating stepping legs. The city is a layer cake of platforms, scaffolding, and staircases that is built atop a steel chassis connected to the leg mechanisms. There are 2 distinct areas in Amsa: buildings which can see the sky, "topside"; and buildings which cannot see the sky, "underside". A building which is on the bottommost layer, but constructed on the outermost shell of Amsa is considered to be part of topside Amsa, while a building towards the top of Amsa, that is concealed from the sky, is "underside Amsa". This differentiation is of particular import to the city's underbelly.

New building proposals, and all material imports, must provide a weight and intention to Pye's office before construction can begin. Since the city is balanced on 3 legs at any one moment in time, it is pivotal that no one side of Amsa is heavier than the others. This building restriction has resulted in an Amsa that is circularly symmetric in weight distribution, but not necessarily symmetric architecturally. Warehouses of bastilite underside may be counterbalanced by an entire wall of casinos, brothels, and housing topside.
## Societal structures
Amsa's isolated nature makes it an ideal city of vice, and its disconnect from Revanon's greater society makes it a safe haven for all sorts of unsavory folk. The physical divide between topside and underside Amsa corresponds to a social divide between the two sets of citizens. Topside Amsa is largely populated by eccentric artificers; shopkeepers peddling mechanical-technological wares; casino operators and high-rollers; elites enjoying a desert vacation; and other particularly wealthy citizens. Underside Amsa is largely populated by the mass of workers who've been tasked with keeping the place running; refugees, once seeking safe harbor, now with no place to turn; drug, gambling addicts; people lauded with steep debts to any number of Amsa's criminal underbelly; Amsa's criminal underbelly. 

Amsa, politically, was divided into 5 parts: allegiance to [[Eternos]], the [[Outfit]], the [[Weave]], the local Thieves' Guild chapter, or to Amsa's government itself. Citizens in underside Amsa were forced, in one way or another, to participate in 1 of the 4 gangs, for protection, for money, or for safety. Citizens in topside Amsa were largely ushered under the wing of Amsa's official civil guard, who labor to upkeep Amsa's status quo.

Following the Weave's destruction of the Outfit in the course of ETERNITY, and Dante's bombing of Eternos' HQ (and the surrounding area) during SOLSTICE, the power structures within underside Amsa have all but fallen to bits. In response, the Amsan civil guard has ramped up its efforts in quashing the underside, and begun anxiously policing her citizens.

Some underside have taken up arms in attempts to re-conquer Amsa's underbelly. Nero du Fleur-Donati gathered the remains of the Weave into [[Arachnis]], a revival sect of the Weave currently busy with recruitments, establishing ties into the civil guard, and settling territorial lines by making friends of the locale. Jaxon Jet of Eternos claims to have witnessed Dante bombing Eternos, and is leading similar efforts to Nero. Additionally, influence from the Shadowfell has spread into Amsa's legs post-Solstice, forcing Amsa's civil guard to maintain a heightened military presence at each leg juncture. Lastly, the [[Unseen]] have found it prudent to gain power in Amsa; Ambrose's successor, Five, has made it a point to stake a claim to the walking city. Five has moved to coerce the richest operators in Amsa, towards the ends of exploiting the flow of capital in order to indirectly manage Amsans. 
## Corcoran Laboratory
Corcoran Laboratory is situated in topside Amsa, and focuses its efforts on the research and development of genetic mutations, and evolutionary branching. Corcoran Laboratory was temporarily taken by force by [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], in the lead-up to SOLSTICE with the explicit goal of developing bio-weapons capable of dominating the world. Following the SOLSTICE campaign, Corcoran Laboratory was liberated from Tangmazu's influence, and business as usual resumed.
## Green Bay Plaza
Green Bay Plaza is a square constructed on topside Amsa, an uncharacteristically clean and formal area of the walking city. The Plaza is a horseshoe-shaped rink, with food stands, entertainment, and all sorts of party-enabling accoutrement. The hole in the center is rimmed with an automated anti-gravity net, allowing Plaza visitors to leap off into the desert, and be whisked safely back to where they leapt. The Plaza is surrounded on all sides by aggressive fans and air conditioning, covered in flowing silks and generous awnings, and provides denizens with a sumptuous view of the desert.
## History
- In the SOLSTICE campaign, the Green Bay Plaza hosted the 3rd annual Diver Symposium, a celebration of advancements made in engineering technology. During this Symposium, the party of SOLSTICE made contact with [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], who was in attendance while courting the favor of [[Eternos]] and their engineering minds.
