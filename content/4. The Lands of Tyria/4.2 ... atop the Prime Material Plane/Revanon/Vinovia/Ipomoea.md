Ipomoea /ˌɪpəˈmiː.ə/ is a city-state ruled by the warlord [[Chrysant Estera]]. Its citizen live in buildings constructed out of the lush forests and caves adorning the inside of [[Ipomoea Canyon]]. Ipomoea's laws distinguish between [[Trueborn]] and [[Lifetouched]]. Ipomoea is the heart of Chrysant Estera's growing desert empire.
