Lex Lutetia is a holding facility built atop a cliff overlooking the northeastern shore of [[Vinovia]]. Lex Lutetia accepts long-term prisoners from cities like [[Amsa]], and uses their labor to achieve a variety of ends. [[Qatar]] was sent to Lex Lutetia following his surrender at Lex Proxima. He broke out, shortly after his arrival at Lex Lutetia, much to the chagrin of the warden, Adina.
