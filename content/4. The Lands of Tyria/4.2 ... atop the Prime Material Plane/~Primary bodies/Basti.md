Basti is the second largest continent in the [[Material Plane]], and is home to a wide variety of disparate, individual habitations. Basti is most famous on a Tyriannic-scale for its production and export of [[Bastilite]], a unique alloy forged from the locally produced bastilium.

Basti possesses a long and storied history of cults, religious fanatics, and strict devotion to [[Lunaris]]. The unique magical properties of bastilium inspired the early Bast (read: person from Basti) to form deeply religious beliefs around the bastilium. Ancestral Bast saw themselves as god-blessed, given dominance over this unique material to serve a greater purpose -- the shape of which, transformed over centuries of history.
## History
Early Bastian civilization centered itself upon those who could tame the bastilium into a useable, commoner-friendly form. While elves and humans with magical propensities could, in practice, use the raw bastilite to their advantage, it was the early greensmiths who could forge metal from the bastilite, that were worshipped. Theirs was a gift from Torag, who directed the early Bast through their greensmiths' hammers to greatness, and godliness.

Following the fall of Hasha, Tangmazu, the trickster's lies spread overseas, and denizens of Basti began to hear the word of Lunaris. Some dissented, holding devout to the word of the dwarven god; others listened, falling into rank and file behind Lunaris' guidance; others still would become enamored with bastilium's magical properties, and form their own city, the Mirage City.

The Basti of the 100s-400s is one dominated by religious strife, holy wars over possession of bastilium, the isolation of those disenchanted with the gods, and the rise of a central power in Basti. Mayhausen Keep, a pivotal hold on the central lands of Basti, rose in worship of Lunaris. Sondra of the Moon lay claim to its creation, painted in memos and recounting as a guardian of Lunaris, and a devout protector of her people. Mayhausen Keep was to be a bastion for dissenting workers fleeing the "indenturement of Baratrum"; a place of freedom from Torag's "backwards thinking".

Following the Solstice, Basti continues to be embroiled in religious fervor. The seeds of doubt, sown by the actions of the Dreamwalkers, and the heresy from Mania overseas, weakened the religiosity that so consumed Basti. Mayhausen Keep entered a period of inquisition, and questioned their fundamentals. The Dwarves of Baratrum Peaks found a foothold at the continental table. All, found themselves united-at-arms in the struggle against the Shadowfell, and all equally found themselves confounded by the changes the Realm of Chaos wrought.
