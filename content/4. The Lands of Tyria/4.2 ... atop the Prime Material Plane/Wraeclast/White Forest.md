The White Forest was a region adjacent to and surrounding [[Lioneye's Watch]]. The White Forest was named such following [[Arakaali]]'s entering into the area, and subsequent spawning of thousands of territorial spiders. The White Forest, prior to [[Wraeclast]]'s sinking, was a forest covered with spiderwebs so dense and layered, that the leaves appeared white. Following Torentia's severance of Wraeclast from Tyria, the White Forest was sunk into the Unending Sea.
