The Maelström is an everlasting storm of chaotic energy, and is currently thought to exist at one of the limits of [[Planar cosmology|stellar depth]], with the [[Void]] at the other end. 

Like the Void, nothing returns from the Maelström. Aetheric phenomena that depend on spatial relationships, like teleportation or blinking, break down near the Maelström, and its gravitational pull is enough to condemn those who fly too near to death.
## Maelström and the center of the universe
The Maelström is, at time of writing, theorized to lie at the center of the universe. While there is merit to placing the Void at the center of the universe, [[Luminiferous Aether|aetheric theory]] favors the Maelström over the Void.

Aetheric theory predicts that any single aetheric phenomena wants to move away from any other aetheric phenomena. As was observed in the [[Realm of Chaos]] pre-[[Solstice]], blasts of deadly aetheric energy would fire out of the Maelström, occasionally striking islands in the Realm--and rarely--destroying them. If aetheric theory is to be accepted, (and the Maelström is *not* to be believed to be sentient,) then it follows that highly charged aetheric energy was being physically repelled out of the Maelström and into the Realm by random chance. 

Given that we have yet to discover a source of aetheric charge as monolithic as the Maelström, it stands to reason that the Maelström is the dominant source of all aetheric flow. Nothing in the universe has as much raw energy swirling around in one place as there is in the Maelström. Even if the universe had managed to spawn in some other location, for all intents and purposes, the Maelström now functions as its stellar center, and its aetheric center.
## Maelström and Celestia
While [[Alethia]] can be accessed by sailing down the rivers Acheron or Cocytus, Celestia has yet to be reached by physical means. A popular theory places Celestia at the heart of the Maelström, positioning the Maelström as Celestia's protector. Notably, it's similarly possible for Celestia to be placed at the heart of the Void, although this is a much less agreeable and indeed less popular theory.
## Beyond the Maelström?
A popular theory places the far boundary of the Void adjacent to the far boundary of the Maelström. This would answer the question, "what lies beyond the Void/Maelström?", with: "The Maelström/Void."
