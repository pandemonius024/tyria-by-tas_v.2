Celestia is the dreamscape from which [[Tyria]] awoke following the death of her children, the [[Divinarius]]. It is a realm of memories, populated by the most impactful, most memorable, and most characterful individuals from the Prime Material Plane. The gods and powers of Tyria often live in Celestia, atop the [[Mount Celestia]], or in [[Calistria]] around the Mount.

Qatar Altan inherited Selûne, the enervate's shrine to the Light, a three-building establishment atop a hill overlooking Calistria. There, he uses a scrying pool and divininate powers to subtly influence the course of Tyrannic history.

A talking gazebo named Erastil is built lakeside, on the outskirts of Calistria. It spoke to Dante and Qatar during their brief visit to Calistria in the Eclipsed timeline, and informed them that they were in Celestia, and where to find Calistria. It also informed them that its maker's name is Avistani. 

Celestia as a plane is not-oft returned from. For those mortal souls who are lucky enough to become immortalized in its memory, leaving Celestia often isn't even considered. For the mortal souls who aren't intended to be in Celestia, a gateway to banishment lies underneath the Mount Celestia. There, a huge rift in the plane is kept open, constantly attended to by Calistrians, and used by Dante and Qatar in their bid to return home.
