Kitava is a creature whose sole aim is to consume all life. Kitava came into existence in celestial opposition to the unyielding hopefulness that [[Aris, the unwritten]] has come to embody. Kitava's hunger is boundless and insatiable. Kitava could not be satisfied by its brethren nor village, neither its house nor its people. Kitava is a building-sized humanoid bearing an "X" slashed onto its face. Its skin is charred black like a roast left turning over an open flame for weeks.
## History
- In a time immemorial, Kitava was sealed away.
- Kalarel, the Vile succeeded at undoing the seal by tempting Kitava with a tower filled with corpses.
- Soon after the [[Solstice]], [[Torentia]] deemed it a worthy trade-off to sink the continent of [[Wraeclast]] deep into the water, leaving Kitava to drink from an endless spigot from the [[Plane of Water]]. 
- In the Eclipsed timeline, Kitava is never sunk into the ocean, and is left to consume life on Wraeclast with wanton disregard.
