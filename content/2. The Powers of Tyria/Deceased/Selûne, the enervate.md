Selûne was the god of the [[Light]], a herald that watched over Tyria and her creations. Selûne commanded dominion over the forces of Good and Law that streamed in from the [[Outer Sphere Planes]]. Selûne lived in [[Celestia]], enacting her will upon Tyria through visions and scrying. In life, Selûne was often found sporting pale white robes, silver adornments, and carrying herself with weightlessness and brevity. 

At the end of the SOLSTICE campaign, Selûne met her end with grace when [[Qatar]] ascended to Celestia to take his place as destined heir of the Light. After centuries spent in tireless penance, Selûne had eventually decided that it was time for another to take her place. By communing with her chosen, [[Isko Kidar]], Selûne destined her offspring, Qatar, for godhood -- and herself, to an end.
