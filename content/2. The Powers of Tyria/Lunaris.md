Lunaris is the embodiment of the moon over [[Tyria]], dancing forevermore across the [[Prime Material Plane]] with her sister, [[Solaris]]. 

As a mortal, Lunaris would venerate the night sky with levity, dance, song, and companionship; the moon itself with quiet prayer and art. Her nightly worship gained her the love and admiration of her people, elevating her in the eyes of Tyria. Lunaris was often avoidant of her younger sister, Solaris, preferring instead to spend time with herself and her various partners. Lunaris' life as a mortal was one of penance and worship; of the moon, of the night, and of the land.

Lunaris, like Solaris, is deeply impacted by the sentiment and belief of her clergy. When [[Tangmazu, the trickster]] spent centuries disseminating the story that Lunaris and Solaris were locked in an eternal duel, not dancing together across the sky, Lunaris was changed. She came to embody the wishes of her people -- despite her history of celebratory prayer.

The largest congregation in worship of this new image of Lunaris was at [[Mayhausen Keep]]. This congregation used her image to encourage unity and warring across [[Basti]], which gave Lunaris a disposition to be violent and aggressive towards Solaris.
## History
- In the Eclipsed timeline, Lunaris laughed triumphantly while Solaris melted onto Basti.
- Later in the Eclipsed timeline, Lunaris was slain by the ECLIPSE party, leaving her corpse atop northwestern Asteria.
- Later in the Eclipsed timeline, during the SOULREND campaign, Lunaris' moonstone was discovered -- twice. [[Eliza Khione]]'s mother, and later, Eliza herself, each discovered the moonstone during an expedition across the Endless Sea.
- In the Solsticed timeline, the church to Lunaris at Mayhausen Keep collapsed. This collapse, of the single largest congregation in worship of a violent Lunaris, began the slow journey towards undoing Tangmazu's lie.
