Aris is the god of life and all things living, and the patron saint of benevolence in Revanon. The city most devout in their worship of Aris is [[Asteria]], and there, she is commonly hailed as humans' deliverance from chaos and destruction. For those communities and worshippers who follower her, she has an ethereal, omnipotent presence in commoner's lives. Aris is known to heal the sick and wounded when presented to her, whether on church altars, or in the altars of worshippers' homes. Individuals seeking her divine intervention construct any ritual bed they can, adorning the sick with decorative wear, which typically consists of floral necklaces, flowing white gowns, and an undisturbed opening to the skies. A column of light encapsulates those blessed by her benevolence, bringing them from their dire straits to a finer state.

Aris in life was a devout herald of the [[Light]], a nomad from Vinovia who traveled across Revanon to salve the wounded, and aid the fallen. Her journey began in 11 AS, when Aris led the sick out of [[Hasha]]. Recognizing the threat that the [[Brudel]] posed to the weaker city-state, Aris made the decision to evacuate as many sick and wounded from Hasha's hospitals as possible, and seek a better life elsewhere. This began a months' long march west across Vinovia, in which Aris and her gathering weathered the sands and marshes seeking refuge elsewhere. While the journey was exceptionally costly in both lives and resources, the people Aris was able to evacuate were rescued from [[The Day that Hasha Died|the temporal bubble that destroyed Hasha]]. The people Aris rescued, the medics who threw their lives behind her banner, and the citizens whose lives she touched in that march, formed the backbone of worship and religiosity, upon which Aris grew to godliness.

> *"Give me your tired, your poor,  
> Your huddled masses yearning to breathe free,  
> The wretched refuse of your teeming shore.  
> Send these, the homeless, tempest-tost to me,  
> I lift my lamp beside the golden door!"*
