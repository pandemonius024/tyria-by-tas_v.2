Pharasma is the god of death, eraser of memories, and the source of the river Lethe. She resides in [[Alethia]], a plane carved out of the aether, in a spot just out of Tyria's sight. There, Pharasma washes all mortals who grace her realm of their memories, with a purposeful vindication that seems to have been spawned from her tenure in life. These [[Forgotten]] are then either put in the employ of Pharasma in the pursuit of upkeeping Alethia, or are then dissolved into the Aether, to be molded into mortal flesh once more.

Pharasma has a lengthy history as a dark presence in Hasha, constructor of [[E'Lenora]]'s Hill, divider of the Prime Material Plane, and creator of Alethia. Pharasma claimed her throne as god of death following her usurpation of Death. 

In the ECLIPSE campaign, Pharasma summoned [[Mori Tenebris]] to answer Phobos' dying plea to save Deimos' life. Mori, an alternate-reality version of Phobos that never died to Deimos' shadow portals, would then fail to stitch Phobos and Deimos back together, resulting in Pharasma banishing him back to his reality.

Pharasma also established a resurrection deal with [[Damian Ashborne]], of the Soldiers of Strife. Damian, in return for acquiring resurrection, would seek out to slay Mania at Pharasma's behest. 

In the Eclipsed timeline, Damian was sent to the Boneyard following his attempted killing of Selûne, the enervate; in the Solsticed timeline, Damian was permanently slain at E'Lenora.
