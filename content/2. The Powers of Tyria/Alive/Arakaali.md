Arakaali was [[Wraeclast]]'s god of lust, taking the form of a giant spider, and residing in what was the [[White Forest]]. Following the ASCENSION crew sparing Arakaali and her minions, the great spider took over the area containing Lioneye's Watch, as well as the surrounding forest.

Following Torentia's drowning of Kitava, the insatiable, most of Wraeclast and her lands were submerged into the sea. Alongside Wraeclast, Arakaali and her followers were summarily drowned. However, the power that Arakaali drew from her worshippers remained, for a time, and enabled her to survive by skimming on the waters of the Unending Sea.
