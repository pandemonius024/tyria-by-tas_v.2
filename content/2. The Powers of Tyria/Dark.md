The Dark is a primordial force created alongside the [[Light]]. The Light and the Dark are equal and opposite in their dominion and power. For every prayer answered, champion uplifted, and god enthroned under the domain of the Dark, an equivalent one is answered by the Light.

The wishes, dignitaries, and gods uplifted by the Dark are characterized by a crass self-preservation and distinct self-centeredness. Champions and chosen of the Dark are those particularly hard-to-kill, those who preserve their history and themselves above all odds.
