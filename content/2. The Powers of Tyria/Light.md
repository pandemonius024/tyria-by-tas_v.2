The Light is a primordial force created alongside the [[Dark]]. The Dark and the Light are equal and opposite in their dominion and power. For every prayer answered, champion uplifted, and god enthroned under the domain of the Light, an equivalent one is answered by the Dark.

The wishes, dignitaries, and gods uplifted by the Light are characterized by a devout selflessness to the point of self-destruction. Like a candle, champions of the Light have a history of burning themselves out to inspire hope and resilience in the eyes of the world.
