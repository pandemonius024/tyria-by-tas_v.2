The Al'Nuwr (full. Atfal Al'Nuwr, lit. "children of the light") is the [[Light]]'s paramilitary, formed of devotees to [[Qatar]] seeking to enact his will upon the face of Tyria. Devotees seeking to join the Al'Nuwr are required to beseech the approval of the god of the light, before receiving his blessing and being anointed as an *aistahama* -- one who has been "bathed" in the Light. The Al'Nuwr, by Qatar's decree, typically operate in small groups of 3-8 combatants, operating with limited discretion, and under Qatar's watchful eye. While some groups may interact with each other, for the most part the Al'Nuwr operate entirely independently from all other Al'Nuwr, relying solely upon guidance from their god to maintain operational efficiency and enforce norms.
