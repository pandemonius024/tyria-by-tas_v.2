The Ebony Legion was the military arm of the theocratic city state, [[Theopolis]]. The Ebony Legion borne obsidian-black armor, allegedly forged from "burnt metal, hardened to stone". The Legion answered directly to the monarch of Theopolis, and was primarily used in the division, subjugation, and control of [[Oriath]]'s citizens.

Following Torentia's sinking of Wraeclast, the Ebony Legion and any traces of the Legion, have been sunk into the Unending Sea. The Legion, its power structure, and the grand majority of its soldiers, have been destroyed.
