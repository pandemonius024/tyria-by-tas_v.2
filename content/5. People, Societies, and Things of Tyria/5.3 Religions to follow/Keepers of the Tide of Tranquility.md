Founded by [[Eliza Khione]] after she leaped into the Solsticed timeline, the Keepers of the Tide of Tranquility (Tide of Tranquility for short) is a parish nested in [[Cor Scant]]. The Keepers are dedicated to the continued neutrality of nations and people between one another, and work towards providing amnesty and grace to those who need it most.
