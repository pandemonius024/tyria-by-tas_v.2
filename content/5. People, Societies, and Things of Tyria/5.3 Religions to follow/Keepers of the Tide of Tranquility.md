The Keepers of the Tide of Tranquility is an active regiment based out of [[Cor Scant]], and was founded by one [[Eliza Khione|Alasia Silvermoon]]. The Keepers are dedicated to the continued neutrality of nations and people between one another, and work towards providing amnesty and grace to those who need it most.

Their crest is a crescent moon hanging above a fortress tower.

The Keepers of the Tide of Tranquility are guided by the teachings of Alasia Silvermoon. Alasia emphasized nonviolence above all other things, and stressed the use of violence as only a means of returning to a state of nonviolence. Beasts can be coerced, bandits can be intimidated, but an arrow can't be un-shot.

Alasia, and the Keepers as a whole, believe that civilization can only persevere in a state of nonviolence. It is their duty, then, as those trained in the ways of warfare and combat, to learn to use that power for good.
## History
In spring of 636 AS, Alasia Silvermoon arrived in Cor Scant, and defended it from wildlife threats. Afterwards, Alasia remained, and began instructing willing villagers how to survive. A group of fighters had formed by the end of the year; this group would have become the first Keepers.

In the winter of the following year, Alasia's group of fighters ventured out to defend Cor Scant from winter predators. None returned. 

Alasia remained in and around Cor Scant for 4 years, defending it against threats from beyond. During those years of trying to keep Cor Scant safe on her own, resident Cor Scantians became inspired, and would try to take up arms on their own. Eventually, something clicked, and Alasia started the Keepers as a small regiment.

By 673 AS, two generations of Cor Scantians have enjoyed the protection of the Keepers. The Keepers became able to function independently, and extended their reach beyond Cor Scant's borders.
