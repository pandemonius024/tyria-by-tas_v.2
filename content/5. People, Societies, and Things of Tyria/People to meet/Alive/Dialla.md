Dialla is a rogue, secluded researcher studying the [[Tears of Tyria]] in an effort to replicate, and harness their powers. Dialla's primary place of study is inside of the [[Rosholm Caverns]], a cave system snaking adjacent to and underneath [[Highgate]]. Dialla's ultimate goal with the creation and usage of the Tears, is to fashion a set of minions who could exact her will upon the world. The precise form of that will, is yet unknown.

Dialla is responsible for implanting jewels where [[Rox]]' eyes used to be. The sapphire-blue that Dialla forged reflects her best attempt at recreating the Tear of Space and its effects. As a result, Rox achieved personal wormhole-creating abilities, which he used to remain evasive and mobile while progressing his goals on Revanon and Basti. Following Rox' death, the jewels implanted into his body seem not to work, or impart its powers to a casual user. It is not known if replacing one's eyes with Dialla's work, would grant the user Rox' powers.

In the ASCENSION campaign, Dialla faced off with the party deep within her laboratory, resulting in her defeat. The party then chose to parlay with Dialla, ultimately sparing her from death -- in exchange for her cooperation. The party was searching for a man named Shay, and bid Dialla use her instruments to help them find him. Dialla, nefariously, used her instruments not to locate this Shay, but instead secure her escape.
