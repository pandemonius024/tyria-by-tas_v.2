Qiramisu was born a human girl in 612 AS into the humble farming village, [[Icehaven]]. In the year 633 AS, all of Icehaven was scorched to the ground, leaving behind little more than stone walkways and house foundations. Eventually, the villagers remains were piled into a mass grave by Asterian soldiers.

In the year 673 AS, 3 months before AFTERSHOCK met at [[Trogal]], Qiramisu awoke as a revenant. She was surrounded by the frozen corpses of her townsmen, their bodies long-cold and hairless. Her memories were fuzzy and loose; only the outline of her people, the vague faces of her parents and 2 sisters, favored memories from her youth, and limited history about Icehaven remained. That, and the memory of the cold fire that seared her and her people away.

Qiramisu left Icehaven's remains, and began to carve out a new life for herself. She quickly discovered that she had an uncanny affinity with thievery; she used her ill-gotten gains to equip and disguise herself as a more typical human woman. Memories fuddled, her only guiding force was her vengeance mark, as it was already placed before she came to. 

Qiramisu took a job out of Trogal to make her way southwards towards the bearer of her mark -- towards [[F'mdell]].
