King Radovan was the ruler of [[Asteria]] and her realms. As king, Radovan incorporated many villages on the outskirts of [[Metrica Province]] into Asteria's domain; quashed anti-elf sentiments amongst growing separatists; and grew Asteria into a global nexus of trade, arts, and culture.
