Marcel Mikhail is a middle-aged human noble of [[Asteria]], who spends most of his time lounging about, and playing activist investor in Asterian spaces. His wealth has, unbeknownst to him, been acquisitioned by [[Hek]]. Hek was transported out of the Shattered wasteland, directly into his bank vault by Tyria, who holds disdain for Mikhail's politics.

Mikhail became invested in a number of burgeoning small businesses in Asteria, and is most notable for attempting to rescue a failing horse rental service. Situated on the north-western outskirts of Asteria, Ulakian's horse rental was an elf-ran establishment who was facing sour waters. Mikhail, in a bid to help the business, offered his "expertise in horse renting", and harassed Ulakian's employees until they just let him run the business. Subsequently, Mikhail's inability to make money on his own resulted in Ulakian's coffers running dry. In the course of Ulakian's downfall, Mikhail established a horse armor decorating service which Ulakian themselves would later describe as, "not financially conducive to that business' success".
