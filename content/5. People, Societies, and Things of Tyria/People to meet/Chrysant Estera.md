Chrysant Estera is a Vinovian warlord who invested heavily into his own cloning. After learning about [[Tangmazu, the trickster|a legendary sorcerer]]'s cloning experiments at [[Lex Proxima]], Chrysant pursued cloning projects of his own to use as decoys and trusted agents. Chrysant's ambitions were rewarded with numerous failures and letdowns. [[Callian Estera]] is one of those failures.
## History
Following the chaos wrought by the Solstice, the warlord Chrysant Estera seized control of [[Ipomoea]], a small village in the [[Ipomoea Canyon]] in western [[Vinovia]]. By Vitali of 634 AS, he had repurposed Ipomoea into a base for conducting raids throughout the region.

For his ambitious expansionist move, Chrysant was rewarded with an attempt on his life by a fellow warlord, the Wraeclastian [[Eliza Khione|Alasia Silvermoon]]. In 636 AS, Alasia used her arcane prowess to attempt and ambush on Chrysant, which he survived.

In 638 AS, Chrysant learned about the cloning laboratory at Lex Proxima, sparking the idea of creating clones of himself for his personal safety. Chrysant recruited the disgraced Magister of Conjuration [[Pavonius Tigrida]] to this end. Together with Pavonius, Chrysant constructed a secret laboratory of his own underneath Ipomoea, where his followers labored away at creating clones of himself.

Chrysant's cloning project led to numerous failures and letdowns. Without access to the [[Tear of Life]], Chrysant and Pavonius found it incredibly difficult to imbue lifeless clones of Chrysant with any amount of sentience or emotion. Callian Estera is one of these failures. Callian managed to become gender-swapped somehow, and also failed to produce any of the charisma or empathy Chrysant needed in a clone. Failures like Callian were slated for disposal.

In the time since, Chrysant toiled away at managing his ever-expanding conquered lands, maintaining relations with the likes of Alasia and [[Asteria]], and chipping away at making successful clones of himself underneath Ipomoea.
