Parce is the founder of Parce's Parcels, one of the shipping companies responsible for moving items across continental waters. Parce is from the Eclipsed timeline.

Parce is a silver kobold who has lost his left arm. Parce holds the shipping charter of [[Asteria]], and is responsible for working with the other charter holders to move their goods across great distances.
