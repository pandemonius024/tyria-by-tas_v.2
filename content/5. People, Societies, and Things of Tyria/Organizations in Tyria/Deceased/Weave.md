The Weave was an arms dealership and weapons manufacturer based out of the underside of [[Amsa]]. It was originally helmed by the half-Goliath [[Auntie]], a scrawny weapons developer in [[Virgil]], and muscle in [[Dante du Fleur]]. Following a raid by rival gang [[Eternos]] in conjunction with Damian Ashborne's Soldiers of Strife, the Weave succumbed to fatal wounds, leaving only 22 scant survivors. Manetto Donati, sent by Dante in an attempt to defend the Weave, has yet to be found.

In the ETERNITY campaign, the Weave provided intel on Anton Essa's movements, and would ask the party for their help in rescuing Virgil as well. Since Dante was rescued from Eternos by the ETERNITY cast, Auntie and the Weave at large became indebted to the party. They paid this debt by sheltering them from opposition, feeding them, and celebrating their successes with them. At the end of ETERNITY, Agnar had an arm-wrestling contest with Auntie which he wins (thanks in part to Signus' aid), Dante and Virgil finally have a moment to relax, and Qatar Altan is able to submit his spare parts to Virgil for repairs.

In the course of ECLIPSE, the Weave are blindsided by Auntie's disappearance, leaving only a note that guides the party to E'Lenora. Further involvement from the Weave in this timeline is minor.

in the course of SOLSTICE, the Weave, instead, make more direct enemies of Eternos, following the Weave's defeating of the [[Outfit]]. Isolating the remaining two weapons dealers of Amsa, Eternos would then turn to Tangmazu, the trickster for aid. Tangmazu instructed Damian Ashborne to march a number of his Soldiers into the Weave's HQ, alongside Eternos' muscle. 

In preparation for the siege, Dante gifted Virgil a Slate of Distant Letters for use in communication and SOS signals. Virgil, on the last day before their barricades gave way, sent an SOS to Dante. Dante, in response, sent Manetto alone to rescue the Weave. The combined forces would then overpower the Weave and Manetto, obliterating them with brute strength. Auntie would be found dead in her room, with 5+ Soldiers needed to slay her. Virgil was found strangled to death in his laboratory, and Mel, the logistics leader, was speared in the corner of the main hall.

Dante resolved to obliterate Eternos in response. He would travel to Dweomerheart with Cassandra Lumen, develop a bomb to end all bombs, and destroyed the cathedral in which Eternos primarily stayed. With all 3 of the weapons dealers in Amsa destroyed, a vacuum appeared for a new force to arise. Amsa would find that force in Nero du Fleur-Donati's [[Arachnis]], in revenge for the Weave.
