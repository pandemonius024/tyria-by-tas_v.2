Ethos was an [[Axiomites|Axiomite]] general who [[Axis]]' first strike on [[F'mdell]]. Ethos was ambushed by AFTERSHOCK while he was en route to F'mdell to negotiate the terms of their surrender. Ethos was exceedingly overconfident, likely stemming from his perceived superiority over organic lifeforms. Ethos was one of the few remaining Axiomites handcrafted by [[Mania]] herself, and was thought to have a direct link to Mania through unknown means.
