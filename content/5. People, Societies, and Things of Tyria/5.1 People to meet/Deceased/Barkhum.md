A jovial tavernkeeper, and founder of [[Asteria]]'s White Rose, Barkhum was a cornerstone of Asterian society. He stood at just over 6'2", roundly built, and sported a hearty laugh that shook the plates of diners around him. 

Barkhum embodied peace, aimed to quench disagreements between his customers before they turned into conflict, and wished nothing more than to keep all his patrons fed. Often, Barkhum provided meals, free of charge, to those less fortunate in Asteria. This habit of his has gained him the respect of his community, who leapt to his defense at the slightest threat to him.
## History
Barkhum found his roots in [[Southmoor]], where he ran a small tavern of little note. 

In 646 AS, Barkhum passed quietly in his sleep. He named close friend and business partner Gregor Sepp as the new owner of the White Rose.
