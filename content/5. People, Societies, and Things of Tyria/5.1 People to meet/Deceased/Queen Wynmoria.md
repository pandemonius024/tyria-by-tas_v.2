Adelaide Wynmoria was one of [[Dawnguard]]'s monarchs; as monarch, Queen Wynmoria was the firstborn to her father, King Wynmoria. Following her death in 651 AS, Queen Wynmoria was succeeded by Queen Lumsden, who was not a direct offspring of the Queen, but a favored friend of the former monarch. 
## As monarch of Dawnguard
Queen Wynmoria's involvement in Dawnguard's governance followed a trend, beginning with her father, of lessening the reach of the monarchy. As the King had before her, Queen Wynmoria visibly strengthened expectations in the court, delegating tasks like writing and passing tax law down to the lower chambers. While this can be considered a progressive step away from the militant monarchy of Dawnguard's present and past, Dawnguard's citizens remain trapped in war's grasp. Queen Wynmoria's court, populated by favored friends or gold star minorities, such as the [[Warforged]] veteran Orion Ray, continued a Wynmorian legacy of perpetuating violence against [[Elkmire]] and Dawnguard alike.
## History
- In early Eterni of 609 AS, Adelaide Wynmoria was born.
- In 651 AS, Queen Wynmoria came down with a strong fever. In late Azmeri of that year, Queen Wynmoria succumbed to her illness.
