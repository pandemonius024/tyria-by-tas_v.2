Graviar was a dragonborn knight, made into an experimental horror by [[Tangmazu, the trickster]]. He met an early end to a Megaprimatus shortly after being deployed to fight alongside the party from ETERNITY in [[Dino Park]]. When the Tear of Space was used by [[Dante du Fleur]], Graviar's dead body was lifted high into the sky, before it was sent plummeting into the ground.

Later, when certain members of ETERNITY were in [[Alethia]], Graviar plummeted through the aether, and into the river Styx.
