Dante is a dhampir alchemist, an explosives specialist, a weapons expert, and prisoner of the [[Void]]. Dante was once a prominent member of the [[Weave]], developing weapons for them to use as merchandise to sell, and as tools of war to wield. After the Weave collapsed due to external forces, Dante rode out on a blaze of glory, bombing a church and its surroundings out of [[Amsa]], decapitating [[Eternos]] in the process.

When the joint parties of ETERNITY and the Dreamwalkers decided to reforge the bonds of the [[Prime Material Plane]], Dante volunteered himself to perform the act. Immediately afterwards, he beseeched [[Tyria]] for a gateway into the Void, so that he may erase her Tears forevermore. There, he rests, with [[Eternity]].

In his life as a dhampir, Dante was close friends with one [[Virgil]]. Together, they formed the brains behind the weaponry, constantly innovating against evolving threats, always working towards something stronger, more efficient, more deadly. It was Virgil's death at the hands of the corrupted [[Soldiers of Strife]] that sent Dante into his explosive ending.
