Damian Ashborne was the warbishop of the [[Soldiers of Strife]] at the time of their defeat. He was selected by [[Tyrus, the warbringer|Tyrus]] to lead the Soldiers back to a position of military dominance over the region, and had resolved himself to stop at nothing to achieve that.
## Rise and fall
Damian's pursuit of strength above all else began with a reformation of the Soldiers of Strife. Under Damian, the Soldiers' arrangement as a brotherhood of arms shifted. They became focused on appearances and political power. Choice Soldiers were shipped off to foreign nations to act as military advisors; the Soldiers began conducting tours of Revanon. Damian himself wielded authority wantonly and abused his power frequently, famously courting [[Agnar Lightbringer]]'s then-wife, ultimately setting Agnar onto the path of vengeance.

Damian courted more than just Agnar's wife. When [[Tangmazu, the trickster]] was rising to power, Damian happily came to an agreement with him to empower the Soldiers further, at the cost of their enslavement. Damian was willing to work with Tangmazu if it meant that his Soldiers became incorrigible blades.
## History
- Before the timeline split, but after [[Anton Essa]] was slain, Damian crossed paths with Agnar. In another timeline, Agnar sails right down into the convoy and is summarily slaughtered.
- In the Solsticed timeline, at pre-Solstice [[E'Lenora]], Damian faced the combined forces of ETERNITY and the Dreamwalkers. Unable to surmount this devilish force, Damian met his gruesome end, killing each of the enslaved Soldiers in the process.
- In the Eclipsed timeline, ETERNITY intervenes at a later time, and are unable to defend E'Lenora against Damian. Instead, they intercept the warbishop and prevent him from killing [[Selûne, the enervate|Selûne]].
