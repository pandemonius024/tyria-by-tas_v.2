Alurika Reyfina was a middle-aged elven woman living in [[F'mdell]]. She was most notable for her efforts to unify the elven resistance to [[Axis]].
## History
In 551 AS, Alurika was born to parents Yvicia and Emeros Reyfina. Alurika then spent the majority of her life roaming the streets of F'mdell as a volunteer guard, quashing violent spats, defending livestock against wildlife, and dealing with the occasional marauder.

In mid-Vivici (March), 633 AS, Alurika bore witness to the ETERNITY party and the Dreamwalkers uniting off the coast of F'mdell. When her father set out to weave the scene into a grand tapestry, Alurika offered some details of her own.

In mid-Atziri (February), 673 AS, Axis invaded F'mdell, and Alurika rose to the moment. Alurika went door to door, rallying together the elves she knew would not go quietly into occupation, and stood against Axis. Together with the AFTERSHOCK party, Alurika orchestrated a network of scouts, fighters, and engineers capable of resisting Axis.

Not all would fare well for Alurika. As Axis' siege progressed and F'mdell's chances of survival grew dim, Alurika approached [[Ethos]] and surrendered F'mdell in exchange for her peoples' survival. 

Later that night, Alurika was shot dead by [[Callian Estera|an Axiomite assassin]] and [[Qiramisu|its accomplice]]. Alurika's death reignited the resistances' spirit, leading to the death of Ethos -- and the deaths of the 719 elves who stood behind her.
