Throckmorton Normalman was a human politician in [[Asteria]]. Normalman was aiming for the chair of "High Supreme Ruler of the Continent" before his passing. 
## History
Normalman met his demise in the cave system underneath the [[Vardousia Range]], where he and his team met and fought the Rat King of Vardousia. Throckmorton Normalman suffered fatal wounds during this battle, to which he succumbed shortly after slaying the Rat King. For reasons unknown, Normalman refused revival, and his remains have yet to be found.
