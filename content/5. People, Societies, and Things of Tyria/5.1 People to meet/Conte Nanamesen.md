Conte Nanamesen is an elven tea trader in [[F'mdell]]. Conte is the inheritor of the Nanamesen Tea Trading Company, a continental tea trader that imports bricks of dried tea leaves from [[Basti]], and distributes it to purveyors across [[Revanon]]. 

Conte lives a more comfortable life than the average F'mdellian, but a humbler life than their wealth could potentially afford. Conte is annoyingly frugal yet self-rewarding, and viciously protective of their family's tea company. When the Axiomites first pushed into F'mdell, Conte was one of the first to leverage their sway with Asperata ala T'ur to secure their tea leaves, and protect their company.
