Quzahn was a Tabaxi gunslinger, best known for his well-earned arrogance and sharpshooting capabilities. His pragmatism helped bring the Dreamwalkers out of [[Basti]]. 

In his later years, Quzahn spent his time and money running an orphanage for troubled youth in [[Dawnguard]]. There, he taught orphans how to fight and defend themselves against a hostile world; he armed them with dual blades of combat prowess, and survival skills.
