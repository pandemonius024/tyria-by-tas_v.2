Emi R. Tas is a beholder anthropologist based out of [[Dweomerheart]], dedicated to the ongoing research, and compilation of knowledge concerning the denizens of the Prime Material Plane. Emi R. Tas' name comes from the word "emeritus". Emi has produced a number of anthological works, documenting the likes of Revanon's largest desert's history in *VINOVIA*, and a broad covering of individuals and topics of note across Tyria in *Tyria*.
