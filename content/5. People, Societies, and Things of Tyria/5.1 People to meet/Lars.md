Lars is a devil hailing from the [[Boneyard]], who struck a deal with [[Rox]] after his first death, and claimed his soul after his second. Lars resides in the Epiphany, a tower on the eastern reaches of Vinovia that was vacated after ETERNITY slew Anton Essa. Lars worked with Rox from within [[Lex Lutetia]], and once offered to strike a deal with [[Qatar]] for Qatar's escape during Qatar's imprisonment.
