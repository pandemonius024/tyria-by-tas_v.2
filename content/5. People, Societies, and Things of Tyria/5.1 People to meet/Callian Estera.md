Callian Estera /kæːliən ɛˈstʰɛːɹə/ is an improperly-made clone of a Vinovian warlord, [[Chrysant Estera]]. The flaw that made Callian a failure in her creator's eyes is her lack of empathy; when compared to a trueborn creature--someone naturally born, not cloned--she is utterly deficient. She's an adrenaline junkie, and loves gambling. Prior to the AFTERSHOCK campaign, she worked as an independent mercenary, taking on lucrative and otherwise morally-problematic jobs, and traveling from town to town as a guard with the merchant caravans.
## History
In 646 AS, Callian was born into a family of lifetouched clones in Chrysant's cloning laboratory beneath [[Ipomoea]].

In 661 AS, Callian escaped Chrysant's cloning laboratory, and was subsequently taken under the wing of [[Rea Talis]], a former denizen of [[Eliza Khione|Alasia Silvermoon]]'s fiefdom, [[Cor Scant]]. Under Rea's tutelage, Callian became a competent thief and eventual assassin.

In 664 AS, Callian left Ipomoea after Chrysant ordered any and all remaining clones to be gathered up for modification or destruction. Callian then spent the next few years taking up odd jobs and the occasional morally-gray contract.

In 669 AS, Rea finds and contacts Callian for a final favor. Callian was led to believe that this favor would be a simple lift, but ultimately resulted in Callian assassinating the Asterian envoy to Ipomoea. Callian fled.

In 671 AS, Callian met [[Baël Moloch]] in [[Kilhan]]; in Caspiri of 672 AS, Callian met [[Kendal]] in [[Agrizi]].
