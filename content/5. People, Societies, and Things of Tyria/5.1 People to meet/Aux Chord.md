A man of many talents, Aux Chord is the greatest hero that [[Solva]] had ever seen. Aux Chord got his start as a bard with the party of REAVE, having provided morale-boosting song and enemy-obliterating punches. Aux Chord singularly withstood the test of spirit given by a strange woman in a lake, fearlessly stepping into waters that had forced others to turn away in fear. After proving his purity of purpose to Solva's Font of Heroism, Aux Chord was enabled to enter the lake at will, traverse great distances in the blink of an eye, and appear before troubled travelers.

In the ETERNITY campaign, Aux Chord made an appearance before ETERNITY using the Font. The Font took on the form of a desert oasis, and gave the party respite from a sandstorm that they were weathering.

After learning that the plot to resurrect [[Kitava, the insatiable]] was underway, Aux Chord made for [[Wraeclast]] to attempt to face the ravenous god. Tragically, Aux Chord found himself unable to prevent the return of Kitava, and was forced to back down from his noble goal. Aux Chord has continued on his path of heroism ever since.
