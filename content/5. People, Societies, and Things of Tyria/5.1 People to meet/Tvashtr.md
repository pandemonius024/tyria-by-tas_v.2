Tvashtr is a stone dwarf who survived the extinction of the dwarves. He was found cocooned in the depths of [[Mor Proxima]] by ETERNITY, to whom he lent his metalworking skills in thanks. Tvashtr gave [[Agnar Lightbringer]]'s armor a unique trim after being rescued, and pledged himself to ETERNITY's cause. 

After a brief period of being captured and experimented on in [[Corcoran Laboratory]], Tvashtr was again rescued by ETERNITY when they killed [[Tangmazu, the trickster]], resulting in Corcoran losing funding and terminating all ongoing experiments.
