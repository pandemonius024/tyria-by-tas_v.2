Fatima is [[Qatar]]'s ex-girlfriend, and a member of the [[Ja'Almawt]]. She had been born into the tribe, and remains committed to fostering the growth and survival of her people. 

Fatima met Qatar when he was a [[Kalashtar]], and fellow member of the Ja'Almawt. When the Kadir of her generation passed on, Fatima was one of the few that the Kadir had considered to take on his mantle. Fatima declined, citing a desire to remain closer to her people, and live a humbler life. As of 673 AS, Fatima continues to travel with the Ja'Almawt.
