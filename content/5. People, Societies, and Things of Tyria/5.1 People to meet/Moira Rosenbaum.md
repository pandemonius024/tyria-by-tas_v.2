Moira Rosenbaum is an Asterian-based politician, with ties into the [[Unseen]]. During the ECLIPSE and SOLSTICE campaigns, Moira was tasked with the surveillance of [[Deimos Tenebris]] and [[Phobos Tenebris]] by [[Ambrose Tenebris]], to ensure the brothers' safety. Following the Solstice, Moira has refocused her attention on Asterian politics, and is vying for a position of power in the Asterian parliament.
