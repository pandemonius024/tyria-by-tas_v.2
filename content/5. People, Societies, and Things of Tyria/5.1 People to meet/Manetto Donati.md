Manetto Donati is a Fallen Aasimar, and was [[Dante du Fleur]]'s partner. 

After losing Dante to the [[Void]], Manetto turned to the only memory he had left: memories seeking [[Selûne, the enervate]] alongside [[Isko Kidar]]. Manetto embraced this memory, and has since turned to a life of faith under the decree of [[Qatar]].
