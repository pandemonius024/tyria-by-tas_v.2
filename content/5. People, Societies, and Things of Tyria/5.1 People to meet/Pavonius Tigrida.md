Pavonius Tigrida is a [[La Escuela de la Montanha]] graduate, whose degree was revoked by the college after his participation in experiments that used living people. Pavonius was and is motivated to discover what creates life, and what he can do with life. Pavonius sees [[Trueborn]] creatures as inherently superior to [[Lifetouched]] creatures, and justifies his manipulation of living creatures as necessary sacrifices towards solving what he calls a "population crisis".

After losing access to the college's materials and being turned away from numerous cities' magistrates, disgraced Magister of Conjuration Pavonius Tigrida worked with [[Chrysant Estera]] on a cloning project. Disgraced and former M.Cj Pavonius Tigrida was and is responsible for the creation of numerous unstable and dubiously conscious clones of Chrysant Estera. 

After it became clear that Chrysant would fail to create any convincing clones of himself, Pavonius Tigrida began to look elsewhere for methods of creating life itself.
